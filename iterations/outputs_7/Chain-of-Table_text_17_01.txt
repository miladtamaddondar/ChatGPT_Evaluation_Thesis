b. Let $p(t) = a + bt + ct^2$. Then $T(p) = \begin{bmatrix} a - b + c \\ a \\ a + b + c \end{bmatrix}$. The matrix for $T$ relative to the basis $\{1, t, t^2\}$ for $\mathbb{P}_2$ and the standard basis for $\mathbb{R}^3$ is $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -1 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$.
