1. We want to write \( p(t) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3 \) as a linear combination of the basis \( \mathcal{B} = \{1,\, 2t,\, -2+4t^2,\, -12t+8t^3\} \), so set \( 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3 = a\cdot 1 + b\cdot 2t + c(-2+4t^2) + d(-12t+8t^3) \).
2. Expanding the right side: \( a + 2bt + c(-2) + c(4t^2) + d(-12t) + d(8t^3) = a - 2c + (2b - 12d)t + 4c t^2 + 8d t^3 \).
3. Equate coefficients to get the system:\[\begin{cases}a - 2c = 7 \\2b - 12d = -12 \\4c = -8 \\8d = 12\end{cases}\]
4. Write the augmented matrix:\(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c}1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & -12 \\0 & 0 & 4 & 0 & -8 \\0 & 0 & 0 & 8 & 12\end{array}\right]\)
5. Row 3: Divide by 4 to get \( c = -2 \), so\(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c}1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & -12 \\0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\0 & 0 & 0 & 8 & 12\end{array}\right]\)
6. Row 4: Divide by 8 to get \( d = \frac{3}{2} \), so\(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c}1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & -12 \\0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2}\end{array}\right]\)
7. Row 2: Substitute \( d = \frac{3}{2} \) into row 2: \( 2b - 12(\frac{3}{2}) = -12 \implies 2b - 18 = -12 \implies 2b = 6 \implies b = 3 \), so\(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c}1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2}\end{array}\right]\)
8. Row 1: Substitute \( c = -2 \) into row 1: \( a - 2(-2) = 7 \implies a + 4 = 7 \implies a = 3 \), so\(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c}1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2}\end{array}\right]\)
9. The coordinate vector of \( p \) relative to \( \mathcal{B} \) is \( \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{bmatrix} \).
