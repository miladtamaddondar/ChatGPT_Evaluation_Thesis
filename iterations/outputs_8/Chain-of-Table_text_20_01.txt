6. Write the system: $0.3x + 0.3y = 0$, $0.4x + 0.4y = 0$.
7. Both equations reduce to $x + y = 0$, so $y = -x$.
8. Thus, an eigenvector is $\mathbf{v}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix}$.
