1. Let \( U = [\mathbf{u}_1 \ \mathbf{u}_2] = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \); this forms a 3x2 matrix by stacking \(\mathbf{u}_1\) and \(\mathbf{u}_2\) as columns.  
2. Compute \( U^T U \): \( U^T U = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ -\frac{2}{3} & \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \); this is verified by direct multiplication and shows the columns are orthonormal.  
3. Compute \( U U^T \): \( U U^T = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ -\frac{2}{3} & \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} & -\frac{2}{9} & -\frac{4}{9} \\ -\frac{2}{9} & \frac{5}{9} & \frac{4}{9} \\ -\frac{4}{9} & \frac{4}{9} & \frac{5}{9} \end{bmatrix} \); verified by matrix multiplication.  
4. The projection onto \( W \) is \( \mathrm{proj}_W \mathbf{y} = U U^T \mathbf{y} \); this follows from the formula for projecting onto the column space of an orthonormal matrix.  
5. Compute \( U U^T \mathbf{y} \): \( U U^T \mathbf{y} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} & -\frac{2}{9} & -\frac{4}{9} \\ -\frac{2}{9} & \frac{5}{9} & \frac{4}{9} \\ -\frac{4}{9} & \frac{4}{9} & \frac{5}{9} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 8 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{32 - 16 - 4}{9} \\ \frac{-8 + 40 + 4}{9} \\ \frac{-16 + 32 + 5}{9} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{12}{9} \\ \frac{36}{9} \\ \frac{21}{9} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{4}{3} \\ 4 \\ \frac{7}{3} \end{bmatrix} \); verified by direct multiplication.  
6. Final answer: \( U^T U = I \), \( U U^T = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} & -\frac{2}{9} & -\frac{4}{9} \\ -\frac{2}{9} & \frac{5}{9} & \frac{4}{9} \\ -\frac{4}{9} & \frac{4}{9} & \frac{5}{9} \end{bmatrix} \), and \( \mathrm{proj}_W \mathbf{y} = (U U^T)\mathbf{y} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{4}{3} \\ 4 \\ \frac{7}{3} \end{bmatrix} \).
