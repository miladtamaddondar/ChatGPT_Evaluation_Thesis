1. Write the augmented matrix for the system $\begin{cases} x_1 + x_2 = 3 \\ 4x_1 + 5x_2 = 8 \end{cases}$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 1 & 3 \\ 4 & 5 & 8 \end{array}\right]$.
2. Subtract $4$ times row 1 from row 2: $R_2 \to R_2 - 4R_1$, resulting in $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 1 & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 \end{array}\right]$.
3. Subtract row 2 from row 1: $R_1 \to R_1 - R_2$, resulting in $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 \end{array}\right]$.
4. The solution is $\mathbf{x} = \begin{pmatrix} 7 \\ -4 \end{pmatrix}$.
