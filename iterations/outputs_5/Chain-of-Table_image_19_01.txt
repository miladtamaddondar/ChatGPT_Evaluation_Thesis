2. Compute $T(\mathbf{b}_1) = A\mathbf{b}_1 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$.
3. Set up the equation $\begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix} = c_1\begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} + c_2\begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix}$, or $\begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}c_1 + 5c_2 \\ c_1 + 4c_2\end{bmatrix}$.
4. Set up the augmented matrix: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 2 \\ 1 & 4 & 2\end{array}\right]$.
5. Subtract row 1 from row 2: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 2 \\ 0 & -1 & 0\end{array}\right]$.
6. Multiply row 2 by $-1$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0\end{array}\right]$.
7. Subtract $5$ times row 2 from row 1: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0\end{array}\right]$.
8. So $c_1 = 2$, $c_2 = 0$, thus $[T(\mathbf{b}_1)]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 0\end{bmatrix}$.
9. Compute $T(\mathbf{b}_2) = A\mathbf{b}_2 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix}$.
10. Set up the equation $\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix} = d_1\begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} + d_2\begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix}$, or $\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}d_1 + 5d_2 \\ d_1 + 4d_2\end{bmatrix}$.
11. Set up the augmented matrix: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 9 \\ 1 & 4 & 7\end{array}\right]$.
12. Subtract row 1 from row 2: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 9 \\ 0 & -1 & -2\end{array}\right]$.
13. Multiply row 2 by $-1$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 9 \\ 0 & 1 & 2\end{array}\right]$.
14. Subtract $5$ times row 2 from row 1: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2\end{array}\right]$.
15. So $d_1 = -1$, $d_2 = 2$, thus $[T(\mathbf{b}_2)]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix}-1 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$.
16. Therefore, the $\mathcal{B}$-matrix for $T$ is $\boxed{\begin{bmatrix}2 & -1 \\ 0 & 2\end{bmatrix}}$.
