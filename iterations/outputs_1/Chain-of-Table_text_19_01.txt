1. Write the problem as: Find the matrix $[T]_{\mathcal{B}}$ such that $[T(\mathbf{x})]_{\mathcal{B}} = [T]_{\mathcal{B}}[\mathbf{x}]_{\mathcal{B}}$ for all $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^2$.
2. Compute $T(\mathbf{b}_1) = A\mathbf{b}_1 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$.
3. Express $T(\mathbf{b}_1) = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$ as a linear combination of $\mathbf{b}_1$ and $\mathbf{b}_2$: Set $a\mathbf{b}_1 + b\mathbf{b}_2 = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$, i.e., $a\begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} + b\begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$.
4. Set up the augmented matrix for this system: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 2 \\ 1 & 4 & 2\end{array}\right]$.
5. Subtract row 1 from row 2: $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - R_1$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 2 \\ 0 & -1 & 0\end{array}\right]$.
6. Divide row 2 by $-1$: $R_2 \leftarrow -R_2$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0\end{array}\right]$.
7. Subtract $5 \times$ row 2 from row 1: $R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - 5R_2$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0\end{array}\right]$.
8. Solution: $a = 2$, $b = 0$; so $T(\mathbf{b}_1) = 2\mathbf{b}_1 + 0\mathbf{b}_2$.
9. Compute $T(\mathbf{b}_2) = A\mathbf{b}_2 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix}$.
10. Express $T(\mathbf{b}_2) = \begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix}$ as a linear combination of $\mathbf{b}_1$ and $\mathbf{b}_2$: $a\begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} + b\begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix}$.
11. Set up the augmented matrix: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 9 \\ 1 & 4 & 7\end{array}\right]$.
12. Subtract row 1 from row 2: $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - R_1$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 9 \\ 0 & -1 & -2\end{array}\right]$.
13. Divide row 2 by $-1$: $R_2 \leftarrow -R_2$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 9 \\ 0 & 1 & 2\end{array}\right]$.
14. Subtract $5 \times$ row 2 from row 1: $R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - 5R_2$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2\end{array}\right]$.
15. Solution: $a = -1$, $b = 2$; so $T(\mathbf{b}_2) = -1\mathbf{b}_1 + 2\mathbf{b}_2$.
16. Therefore, the $\mathcal{B}$-matrix for $T$ is $[T]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix}2 & -1 \\ 0 & 2\end{bmatrix}$.
