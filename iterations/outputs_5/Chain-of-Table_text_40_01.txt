1. Write each vector in $\mathcal{B}$ in terms of the standard basis: $1 - 3t^2 = (1, 0, -3)$, $2 + t - 5t^2 = (2, 1, -5)$, $1 + 2t = (1, 2, 0)$, so the change-of-coordinates matrix is $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 \\ -3 & -5 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.
2. To write $t^2$ as a linear combination, set up the equation: $c_1(1 - 3t^2) + c_2(2 + t - 5t^2) + c_3(1 + 2t) = t^2$, which gives the augmented matrix $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 0 \\ -3 & -5 & 0 & 1 \end{array}\right]$.
3. Add $3$ times row 1 to row 3: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 3 & 1 \end{array}\right]$.
4. Subtract row 2 from row 3: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 \end{array}\right]$.
5. Subtract $2$ times row 3 from row 2: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 \end{array}\right]$.
6. Subtract row 3 from row 1: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 2 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 \end{array}\right]$.
7. Subtract $2$ times row 2 from row 1: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 \end{array}\right]$.
8. Therefore, $t^2 = 3(1 - 3t^2) - 2(2 + t - 5t^2) + 1(1 + 2t)$.
