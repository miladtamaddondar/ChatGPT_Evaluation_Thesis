1. To find the characteristic polynomial, compute \( \det\left(\begin{bmatrix}2 & 1 \\ -1 & 4\end{bmatrix} - \lambda \begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1\end{bmatrix}\right) = 0 \), which gives \( \det\left(\begin{bmatrix}2-\lambda & 1 \\ -1 & 4-\lambda\end{bmatrix}\right) = 0 \).
2. Calculate the determinant: \( (2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) - (-1)(1) = (2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) + 1 \).
3. Expand: \( (2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) + 1 = 8 - 2\lambda - 4\lambda + \lambda^2 + 1 = \lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9 \).
4. The characteristic polynomial is \( \boxed{\lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9} \).
5. Set the characteristic polynomial to zero: \( \lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9 = 0 \).
6. Factor: \( (\lambda - 3)^2 = 0 \).
7. The eigenvalues are \( \boxed{\lambda = 3} \) (with multiplicity 2).
