1. Start with the columns of the matrix as vectors: \(\mathbf{a}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\), \(\mathbf{a}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix}\), \(\mathbf{a}_3 = \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ 7 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\).  
2. Set \(\mathbf{u}_1 = \mathbf{a}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\).  
3. Compute projection of \(\mathbf{a}_2\) onto \(\mathbf{u}_1\): \(\mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_1} \mathbf{a}_2 = \frac{\mathbf{a}_2 \cdot \mathbf{u}_1}{\mathbf{u}_1 \cdot \mathbf{u}_1} \mathbf{u}_1 = \frac{2(1) + 1(-1) + 4(-1) + (-4)(1) + 2(1)}{1^2 + (-1)^2 + (-1)^2 + 1^2 + 1^2} \mathbf{u}_1 = \frac{2 - 1 - 4 - 4 + 2}{5} \mathbf{u}_1 = \frac{-5}{5} \mathbf{u}_1 = -1 \cdot \mathbf{u}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix}\).  
4. Compute \(\mathbf{u}_2 = \mathbf{a}_2 - \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_1} \mathbf{a}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix}\).  
5. Compute projection of \(\mathbf{a}_3\) onto \(\mathbf{u}_1\): \(\mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_1} \mathbf{a}_3 = \frac{\mathbf{a}_3 \cdot \mathbf{u}_1}{\mathbf{u}_1 \cdot \mathbf{u}_1} \mathbf{u}_1 = \frac{5(1) + (-4)(-1) + (-3)(-1) + 7(1) + 1(1)}{5} \mathbf{u}_1 = \frac{5 + 4 + 3 + 7 + 1}{5} \mathbf{u}_1 = \frac{20}{5} \mathbf{u}_1 = 4 \mathbf{u}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ 4 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix}\).  
6. Compute projection of \(\mathbf{a}_3\) onto \(\mathbf{u}_2\): \(\mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_2} \mathbf{a}_3 = \frac{\mathbf{a}_3 \cdot \mathbf{u}_2}{\mathbf{u}_2 \cdot \mathbf{u}_2} \mathbf{u}_2 = \frac{5(3) + (-4)(0) + (-3)(3) + 7(-3) + 1(3)}{3^2 + 0^2 + 3^2 + (-3)^2 + 3^2} \mathbf{u}_2 = \frac{15 + 0 - 9 - 21 + 3}{9 + 0 + 9 + 9 + 9} \mathbf{u}_2 = \frac{15 - 9 - 21 + 3}{36} \mathbf{u}_2 = \frac{-12}{36} \mathbf{u}_2 = -\frac{1}{3} \mathbf{u}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix}\).  
7. Compute \(\mathbf{u}_3 = \mathbf{a}_3 - \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_1} \mathbf{a}_3 - \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_2} \mathbf{a}_3 = \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ 7 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ 4 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 5-4-(-1) \\ -4-(-4)-0 \\ -3-(-4)-(-1) \\ 7-4-1 \\ 1-4-(-1) \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ -2 \end{bmatrix}\).  
8. Therefore, an orthogonal basis for the column space is \(\left\{ \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ -2 \end{bmatrix} \right\}\).
