5. Add yet another nonzero multiple of the first row to the fourth row, maintaining rank 1: \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 2 & 4 & 6 \\ -1 & -2 & -3 \\ 5 & 10 & 15 \end{bmatrix}\).
