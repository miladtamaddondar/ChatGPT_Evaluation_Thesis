1. Write the augmented matrix for the homogeneous system $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.

2. Subtract $2$ times row 1 from row 3: $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 2R_1$ gives $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -8 & 6 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.

3. Subtract $2$ times row 2 from row 3: $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 2R_2$ gives $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.

4. Write the equivalent system: $x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$, $x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0$.

5. Express $x_1$ and $x_2$ in terms of free variables $x_3 = s$, $x_4 = t$: $x_2 = 4s - 3t$, $x_1 = 4x_2 - 7x_3 + 5x_4 = 4(4s - 3t) - 7s + 5t = 16s - 12t - 7s + 5t = 9s - 7t$.

6. The solution set is $\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4\end{bmatrix} = s\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0\end{bmatrix} + t\begin{bmatrix}-7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}$ for $s, t \in \mathbb{R}$.
