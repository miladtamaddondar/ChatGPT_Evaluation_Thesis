1. Since \( T \) is linear and maps \( \mathbf{e}_1 \) to \( \mathbf{y}_1 \) and \( \mathbf{e}_2 \) to \( \mathbf{y}_2 \), the standard matrix \( A \) of \( T \) has columns \( \mathbf{y}_1 \) and \( \mathbf{y}_2 \): \( A = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \).  
2. To find the image of \( \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} \), compute \( A \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} = 5\mathbf{y}_1 + (-3)\mathbf{y}_2 \) by linearity.  
3. Calculate \( 5\mathbf{y}_1 = 5 \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 10 \\ 25 \end{bmatrix} \) and \( -3\mathbf{y}_2 = -3 \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ -18 \end{bmatrix} \).  
4. Add the results: \( \begin{bmatrix} 10 \\ 25 \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ -18 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 13 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} \).  
5. The image of \( \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} \) under \( T \) is \( \begin{bmatrix} 13 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} \).  
6. For a general vector \( \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} \), \( T\left(\begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix}\right) = x_1\mathbf{y}_1 + x_2\mathbf{y}_2 \) by linearity.  
7. Substitute \( \mathbf{y}_1 \) and \( \mathbf{y}_2 \): \( x_1\begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} + x_2\begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2x_1 - x_2 \\ 5x_1 + 6x_2 \end{bmatrix} \).  
8. The image of \( \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} \) under \( T \) is \( \begin{bmatrix} 2x_1 - x_2 \\ 5x_1 + 6x_2 \end{bmatrix} \).
