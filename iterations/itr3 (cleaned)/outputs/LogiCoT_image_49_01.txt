1. The problem asks for an upper triangular matrix \( R \) such that \( A = QR \), where \( Q \) is given as an orthonormal matrix obtained from the Gram-Schmidt process applied to the columns of \( A \).  
2. To find \( R \), use the formula \( R = Q^T A \) since \( Q \) is orthonormal (\( Q^T Q = I \)), and verify that \( R \) is upper triangular.  
3. Compute \( Q^T \): \( Q^T = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{5}{6} & \frac{1}{6} & -\frac{3}{6} & \frac{1}{6} \\ -\frac{1}{6} & \frac{5}{6} & \frac{1}{6} & \frac{3}{6} \end{bmatrix} \).  
4. Multiply \( Q^T \) and \( A \): \( R = Q^T A = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{5}{6} & \frac{1}{6} & -\frac{3}{6} & \frac{1}{6} \\ -\frac{1}{6} & \frac{5}{6} & \frac{1}{6} & \frac{3}{6} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 5 & 9 \\ 1 & 7 \\ -3 & -5 \\ 1 & 5 \end{bmatrix} \).  
5. Compute the first row of \( R \): \( \left( \frac{5}{6}, \frac{1}{6}, -\frac{3}{6}, \frac{1}{6} \right) \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 1 \\ -3 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \frac{25}{6} + \frac{1}{6} + \frac{9}{6} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{36}{6} = 6 \), and \( \left( \frac{5}{6}, \frac{1}{6}, -\frac{3}{6}, \frac{1}{6} \right) \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 7 \\ -5 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} = \frac{45}{6} + \frac{7}{6} + \frac{15}{6} + \frac{5}{6} = \frac{72}{6} = 12 \).  
6. Compute the second row of \( R \): \( \left( -\frac{1}{6}, \frac{5}{6}, \frac{1}{6}, \frac{3}{6} \right) \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 1 \\ -3 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = -\frac{5}{6} + \frac{5}{6} -\frac{3}{6} + \frac{3}{6} = 0 \), and \( \left( -\frac{1}{6}, \frac{5}{6}, \frac{1}{6}, \frac{3}{6} \right) \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 7 \\ -5 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} = -\frac{9}{6} + \frac{35}{6} -\frac{5}{6} + \frac{15}{6} = \frac{36}{6} = 6 \).  
7. Thus, \( R = \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 12 \\ 0 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \), which is upper triangular as required.  
8. Verification: Multiply \( Q \) and \( R \): \( \begin{bmatrix} \frac{5}{6} & -\frac{1}{6} \\ \frac{1}{6} & \frac{5}{6} \\ -\frac{3}{6} & \frac{1}{6} \\ \frac{1}{6} & \frac{3}{6} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 12 \\ 0 & 6 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 5 & 9 \\ 1 & 7 \\ -3 & -5 \\ 1 & 5 \end{bmatrix} = A \), confirming the solution.
