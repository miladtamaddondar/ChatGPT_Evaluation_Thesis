1. Subtract \( 4I \) from \( A \) to form \( A - 4I \): \(\begin{bmatrix} 3-4 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 1 & 3-4 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 1-4 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 4-4 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}\).
2. Write the augmented matrix for the homogeneous system \((A-4I)\vec{x} = 0\): \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).
3. Add row 1 to row 2: \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).
4. Add row 2 to row 3: \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).
5. Multiply row 1 by \(-1\) and row 2 by \(-1\): \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).
6. Write the system: \(x_1 - 2x_3 = 0\), \(x_2 - 3x_3 = 0\), \(x_3\) free, \(x_4\) free.
7. Express variables in terms of free variables: \(x_1 = 2x_3\), \(x_2 = 3x_3\), \(x_3 = t\), \(x_4 = s\).
8. General solution: \(\vec{x} = \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{bmatrix} = t\begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + s\begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\).
9. The basis for the eigenspace corresponding to \(\lambda = 4\) is \(\left\{ \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \right\}\).
