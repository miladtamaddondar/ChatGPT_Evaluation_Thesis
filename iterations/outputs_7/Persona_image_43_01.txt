1. Write \( U = [\mathbf{u}_1\ \mathbf{u}_2] = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \).  
2. Compute \( U^T U = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{u}_1^T \mathbf{u}_1 & \mathbf{u}_1^T \mathbf{u}_2 \\ \mathbf{u}_2^T \mathbf{u}_1 & \mathbf{u}_2^T \mathbf{u}_2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \) since \( \mathbf{u}_1, \mathbf{u}_2 \) are orthonormal.  
3. Compute \( U U^T = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ -\frac{2}{3} & \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} & -\frac{2}{9} & 0 \\ -\frac{2}{9} & \frac{5}{9} & \frac{4}{9} \\ 0 & \frac{4}{9} & \frac{5}{9} \end{bmatrix} \).  
4. The projection \( \mathrm{proj}_W \mathbf{y} = U U^T \mathbf{y} \).  
5. Compute \( U U^T \mathbf{y} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} & -\frac{2}{9} & 0 \\ -\frac{2}{9} & \frac{5}{9} & \frac{4}{9} \\ 0 & \frac{4}{9} & \frac{5}{9} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 8 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} \cdot 4 + (-\frac{2}{9}) \cdot 8 + 0 \cdot 1 \\ (-\frac{2}{9}) \cdot 4 + \frac{5}{9} \cdot 8 + \frac{4}{9} \cdot 1 \\ 0 \cdot 4 + \frac{4}{9} \cdot 8 + \frac{5}{9} \cdot 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{32}{9} - \frac{16}{9} \\ -\frac{8}{9} + \frac{40}{9} + \frac{4}{9} \\ 0 + \frac{32}{9} + \frac{5}{9} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{16}{9} \\ \frac{36}{9} \\ \frac{37}{9} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{16}{9} \\ 4 \\ \frac{37}{9} \end{bmatrix} \).  
6. The answers are: \( U^T U = I \), \( U U^T = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} & -\frac{2}{9} & 0 \\ -\frac{2}{9} & \frac{5}{9} & \frac{4}{9} \\ 0 & \frac{4}{9} & \frac{5}{9} \end{bmatrix} \), and \( \mathrm{proj}_W \mathbf{y} = (U U^T) \mathbf{y} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{16}{9} \\ 4 \\ \frac{37}{9} \end{bmatrix} \).
