1. Write the matrices \(A = \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 1 & 4 & 5\end{bmatrix}\) and \(D = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 5\end{bmatrix}\).  
2. Compute \(AD\) by multiplying each row of \(A\) by the corresponding diagonal entry of \(D\): \(AD = \begin{bmatrix}1 \times 2 + 1 \times 0 + 1 \times 0 & 1 \times 0 + 1 \times 3 + 1 \times 0 & 1 \times 0 + 1 \times 0 + 1 \times 5 \\ 1 \times 2 + 2 \times 0 + 3 \times 0 & 1 \times 0 + 2 \times 3 + 3 \times 0 & 1 \times 0 + 2 \times 0 + 3 \times 5 \\ 1 \times 2 + 4 \times 0 + 5 \times 0 & 1 \times 0 + 4 \times 3 + 5 \times 0 & 1 \times 0 + 4 \times 0 + 5 \times 5 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 3 & 5 \\ 2 & 6 & 15 \\ 2 & 12 & 25\end{bmatrix}\).  
3. Verify \(AD\) by checking that multiplying by a diagonal matrix on the right scales each column of \(A\) by the corresponding diagonal entry of \(D\): first column by 2, second by 3, third by 5, so \(AD = \begin{bmatrix}1 \times 2 & 1 \times 3 & 1 \times 5 \\ 1 \times 2 & 2 \times 3 & 3 \times 5 \\ 1 \times 2 & 4 \times 3 & 5 \times 5\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 3 & 5 \\ 2 & 6 & 15 \\ 2 & 12 & 25\end{bmatrix}\), confirming correctness.  
4. Compute \(DA\) by multiplying each row of \(A\) by the corresponding diagonal entry of \(D\) (since \(D\) is on the left, it scales the rows): \(DA = \begin{bmatrix}2 \times 1 & 2 \times 1 & 2 \times 1 \\ 3 \times 1 & 3 \times 2 & 3 \times 3 \\ 5 \times 1 & 5 \times 4 & 5 \times 5\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 2 & 2 \\ 3 & 6 & 9 \\ 5 & 20 & 25\end{bmatrix}\).  
5. Verify \(DA\) by checking that multiplying by a diagonal matrix on the left scales each row of \(A\) by the corresponding diagonal entry of \(D\): first row by 2, second by 3, third by 5, so \(DA = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 2 & 2 \\ 3 & 6 & 9 \\ 5 & 20 & 25\end{bmatrix}\), confirming correctness.  
6. Final answers: \(AD = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 3 & 5 \\ 2 & 6 & 15 \\ 2 & 12 & 25\end{bmatrix}\), \(DA = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 2 & 2 \\ 3 & 6 & 9 \\ 5 & 20 & 25\end{bmatrix}\).
