1. Set up the equation $c_1(1+t^2) + c_2(t+t^2) + c_3(1+2t+t^2) = 1 + 4t + 7t^2$ and expand: $c_1(1+t^2) + c_2(t+t^2) + c_3(1+2t+t^2) = c_1 + c_1 t^2 + c_2 t + c_2 t^2 + c_3 + 2c_3 t + c_3 t^2 = (c_1 + c_3) + (c_2 + 2c_3)t + (c_1 + c_2 + c_3)t^2$.
2. Equate coefficients to form the system: $c_1 + c_3 = 1$, $c_2 + 2c_3 = 4$, $c_1 + c_2 + c_3 = 7$.
3. Write the augmented matrix: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 1 & | & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & | & 4 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & | & 7 \end{bmatrix}$.
4. Subtract row 1 from row 3: $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - R_1$: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 1 & | & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & | & 4 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & | & 6 \end{bmatrix}$.
5. Subtract row 2 from row 3: $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - R_2$: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 1 & | & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & | & 4 \\ 0 & 0 & -2 & | & 2 \end{bmatrix}$.
6. Divide row 3 by $-2$: $R_3 \leftarrow \frac{1}{-2}R_3$: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 1 & | & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & | & 4 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & | & -1 \end{bmatrix}$.
7. Eliminate $c_3$ from row 1: $R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - R_3$: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 & | & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & | & 4 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & | & -1 \end{bmatrix}$.
8. Eliminate $c_3$ from row 2: $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 2R_3$: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 & | & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & | & 6 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & | & -1 \end{bmatrix}$.
9. The coordinate vector is $\boxed{\begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 6 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix}}_{\mathcal{B}}$.
