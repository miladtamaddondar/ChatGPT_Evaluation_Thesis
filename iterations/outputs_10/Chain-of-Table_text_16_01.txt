1. Write the effect of $T$ on each basis vector: $T(1) = 3 + 5t$, $T(t) = -2t + 4t^2$, $T(t^2) = t^2$.
2. Express each $T(\text{basis vector})$ as a coordinate vector in $\mathcal{B}$: $T(1) = 3\cdot 1 + 5\cdot t + 0\cdot t^2 \to \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 5 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}$, $T(t) = 0\cdot 1 + (-2)\cdot t + 4\cdot t^2 \to \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ -2 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix}$, $T(t^2) = 0\cdot 1 + 0\cdot t + 1\cdot t^2 \to \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}$.
3. Form the matrix $[T]_{\mathcal{B}}$ by placing these coordinate vectors as columns: $\boxed{\begin{bmatrix} 3 & 0 & 0 \\ 5 & -2 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 & 1 \end{bmatrix}}$.
