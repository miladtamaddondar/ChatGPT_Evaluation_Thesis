1. Write the equation $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$ as an augmented matrix: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.
2. Eliminate the $x_1$ term in row 3 by replacing row 3 with row 3 minus $2 \times$ row 1: Row 3 becomes $[2 - 2 \cdot 1, -6 - 2 \cdot (-4), 6 - 2 \cdot 7, -4 - 2 \cdot (-5), 0 - 2 \cdot 0] = [0, 2, -8, 6, 0]$.
3. The matrix is now $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -8 & 6 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.
4. Eliminate the $x_2$ term in row 3 by replacing row 3 with row 3 minus $2 \times$ row 2: Row 3 becomes $[0, 2 - 2 \cdot 1, -8 - 2 \cdot (-4), 6 - 2 \cdot 3, 0 - 2 \cdot 0] = [0, 0, 0, 0, 0]$.
5. The matrix is now $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0\end{bmatrix}$, which is in row-echelon form and shows the system has two free variables.
6. Write the system as equations: $x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$, $x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0$.
7. Express $x_2$ in terms of $x_3$ and $x_4$: $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$.
8. Substitute $x_2$ into the first equation: $x_1 - 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.
9. Simplify: $x_1 - 16x_3 + 12x_4 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.
10. Combine like terms: $x_1 - 9x_3 + 7x_4 = 0$.
11. Solve for $x_1$: $x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4$.
12. Let $x_3 = s$ and $x_4 = t$ where $s, t \in \mathbb{R}$, then $x_1 = 9s - 7t$, $x_2 = 4s - 3t$, $x_3 = s$, $x_4 = t$.
13. The solution set is $\mathbf{x} = s\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0\end{bmatrix} + t\begin{bmatrix}-7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}$ for $s, t \in \mathbb{R}$.
