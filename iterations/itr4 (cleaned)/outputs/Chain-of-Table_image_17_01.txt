1. To find the image under \( T \) of \( p(t) = 5 + 3t \), evaluate at \( t = -1, 0, 1 \): \( p(-1) = 5 + 3(-1) = 2 \), \( p(0) = 5 + 3(0) = 5 \), \( p(1) = 5 + 3(1) = 8 \), so the image is \( \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 5 \\ 8 \end{array}\right] \).
