1. Write the basis \(\mathcal{B}\) and express \(p(t)\) as a linear combination: Let \(\mathcal{B} = \{1,\, 2t,\, -2 + 4t^2,\, -12t + 8t^3\}\), and set \(p(t) = a_1 \cdot 1 + a_2 \cdot 2t + a_3(-2 + 4t^2) + a_4(-12t + 8t^3)\).  
2. Expand the right side and collect like terms: \(p(t) = a_1 + 2a_2 t - 2a_3 + 4a_3 t^2 - 12a_4 t + 8a_4 t^3\).  
3. Combine like terms for each power of \(t\): \(p(t) = (a_1 - 2a_3) + (2a_2 - 12a_4)t + 4a_3 t^2 + 8a_4 t^3\).  
4. Set up the system by matching coefficients with \(p(t) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3\):  \[\begin{cases}a_1 - 2a_3 = 7 \\2a_2 - 12a_4 = -12 \\4a_3 = -8 \\8a_4 = 12\end{cases}\]  
5. Solve \(4a_3 = -8\) for \(a_3\): \(a_3 = -2\).  
6. Solve \(8a_4 = 12\) for \(a_4\): \(a_4 = \frac{3}{2}\).  
7. Substitute \(a_4\) into \(2a_2 - 12a_4 = -12\): \(2a_2 - 12 \cdot \frac{3}{2} = -12 \implies 2a_2 - 18 = -12 \implies 2a_2 = 6 \implies a_2 = 3\).  
8. Substitute \(a_3\) into \(a_1 - 2a_3 = 7\): \(a_1 - 2(-2) = 7 \implies a_1 + 4 = 7 \implies a_1 = 3\).  
9. The coordinate vector of \(p\) relative to \(\mathcal{B}\) is \(\boxed{\begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{bmatrix}}\).
