1. Express $p(t) = 1 + 4t + 7t^2$ as a linear combination of the basis vectors: $p(t) = a(1 + t^2) + b(t + t^2) + c(1 + 2t + t^2)$.  
2. Expand the right-hand side: $a(1 + t^2) + b(t + t^2) + c(1 + 2t + t^2) = a + a t^2 + b t + b t^2 + c + 2c t + c t^2$.  
3. Combine like terms: $(a + c) + (b + 2c)t + (a + b + c)t^2$.  
4. Set equal to $p(t)$ and equate coefficients: $a + c = 1$, $b + 2c = 4$, $a + b + c = 7$.  
5. Write the system as an augmented matrix: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 1 &|& 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 &|& 4 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 &|& 7 \end{bmatrix}$.  
6. Subtract row 1 from row 3: Row 3 $\to$ Row 3 - Row 1: $[1\ 1\ 1\ |\ 7] - [1\ 0\ 1\ |\ 1] = [0\ 1\ 0\ |\ 6]$.  
7. The updated matrix is $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 1 &|& 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 &|& 4 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 &|& 6 \end{bmatrix}$.  
8. Subtract row 2 from row 3: Row 3 $\to$ Row 3 - Row 2: $[0\ 1\ 0\ |\ 6] - [0\ 1\ 2\ |\ 4] = [0\ 0\ -2\ |\ 2]$.  
9. The updated matrix is $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 1 &|& 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 &|& 4 \\ 0 & 0 & -2 &|& 2 \end{bmatrix}$.  
10. Solve for $c$ from row 3: $-2c = 2 \implies c = -1$.  
11. Substitute $c = -1$ into row 2: $b + 2(-1) = 4 \implies b - 2 = 4 \implies b = 6$.  
12. Substitute $c = -1$ and $b = 6$ into row 1: $a + (-1) = 1 \implies a = 2$.  
13. Thus, the coordinate vector is $\boxed{\begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 6 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix}}$ relative to $\mathcal{B}$.
