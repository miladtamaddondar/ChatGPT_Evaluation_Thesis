1. Compute the dot product $\mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v} = (-1)(4) + (2)(6) = -4 + 12 = 8$.
2. Compute the dot product $\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{v} = (4)^2 + (6)^2 = 16 + 36 = 52$.
3. Compute the scalar $\frac{\mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v}}{\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{v}} = \frac{8}{52} = \frac{2}{13}$.
4. Multiply this scalar by $\mathbf{v}$: $\frac{2}{13} \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{13} \\ \frac{12}{13} \end{bmatrix}$.
5. The answer to part (a) is $\boxed{\begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{13} \\ \frac{12}{13} \end{bmatrix}}$.
6. Compute the dot product $\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{w} = (6)(3) + (-2)(-1) + (3)(-5) = 18 + 2 - 15 = 5$.
7. Compute the dot product $\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{x} = (6)^2 + (-2)^2 + (3)^2 = 36 + 4 + 9 = 49$.
8. Compute the scalar $\frac{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{w}}{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{x}} = \frac{5}{49}$.
9. Multiply this scalar by $\mathbf{x}$: $\frac{5}{49} \begin{bmatrix} 6 \\ -2 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{30}{49} \\ -\frac{10}{49} \\ \frac{15}{49} \end{bmatrix}$.
10. The answer to part (b) is $\boxed{\begin{bmatrix} \frac{30}{49} \\ -\frac{10}{49} \\ \frac{15}{49} \end{bmatrix}}$.
