1. To find the characteristic polynomial, compute \(\det(A - \lambda I)\) for \(A = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 1 \\ -1 & 4\end{bmatrix}\): \(A - \lambda I = \begin{bmatrix}2-\lambda & 1 \\ -1 & 4-\lambda\end{bmatrix}\).
2. The determinant is \((2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) - (1)(-1) = (2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) + 1\).
3. Expanding: \((2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) + 1 = 8 - 2\lambda - 4\lambda + \lambda^2 + 1 = \lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9\).
4. The characteristic polynomial is \(\lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9\).
5. Setting the polynomial to zero: \(\lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9 = 0\).
6. Factoring: \((\lambda - 3)^2 = 0\).
7. The eigenvalues are \(\lambda = 3\) (with algebraic multiplicity 2).
