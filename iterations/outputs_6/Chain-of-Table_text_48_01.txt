1. Set up the system for $\mathbf{v} = \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \\ z \end{bmatrix}$ to be orthogonal to both $\mathbf{u}_1$ and $\mathbf{u}_2$: $x + y - 2z = 0$ and $5x - y + 2z = 0$. The augmented matrix is $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 1 & -2 & 0 \\ 5 & -1 & 2 & 0 \end{array}\right]$.
2. $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 5R_1$: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 1 & -2 & 0 \\ 0 & -6 & 12 & 0 \end{array}\right]$.
3. $R_2 \leftarrow \frac{1}{-6}R_2$: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 1 & -2 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -2 & 0 \end{array}\right]$.
4. $R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - R_2$: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -2 & 0 \end{array}\right]$.
5. The general solution is $x = 0$, $y = 2z$, $z$ free; let $z = 1$, so $\mathbf{v} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 2 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}$.
