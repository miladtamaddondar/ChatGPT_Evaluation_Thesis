1. Write the augmented matrix for the system: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & -2 & -1 \\ -1 & 3 & 4 \\ 3 & -2 & 9 \end{array}\right]$.
2. Add row 1 to row 2: $R_2 \gets R_2 + R_1$, resulting in $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 3 \\ 3 & -2 & 9 \end{array}\right]$.
3. Subtract 3 times row 1 from row 3: $R_3 \gets R_3 - 3R_1$, resulting in $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 3 \\ 0 & 4 & 12 \end{array}\right]$.
4. Subtract 4 times row 2 from row 3: $R_3 \gets R_3 - 4R_2$, resulting in $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]$.
5. Add 2 times row 2 to row 1: $R_1 \gets R_1 + 2R_2$, resulting in $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 0 & 5 \\ 0 & 1 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]$.
6. The solution is $\mathbf{x} = \begin{pmatrix} 5 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix}$.
