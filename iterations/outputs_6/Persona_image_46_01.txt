11. Simplify: \(4x_1 + 4 - 2x_1 + 10 - 2x_1 = 14\).
12. Combine like terms: \((4x_1 - 2x_1 - 2x_1) + (4 + 10) = 14 \implies 0x_1 + 14 = 14\).
