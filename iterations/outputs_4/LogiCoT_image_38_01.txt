1. The basis \(\mathcal{B}\) of \(\mathbb{P}_3\) is given by the Hermite polynomials: \(1, 2t, -2 + 4t^2, -12t + 8t^3\).  
2. We want to write \(p(t) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3\) as a linear combination of the basis: \(p(t) = a_1 \cdot 1 + a_2 \cdot 2t + a_3(-2 + 4t^2) + a_4(-12t + 8t^3)\).  
3. Expand the right side: \(a_1 + 2a_2 t + a_3(-2) + a_3(4t^2) + a_4(-12t) + a_4(8t^3) = a_1 - 2a_3 + 2a_2 t - 12a_4 t + 4a_3 t^2 + 8a_4 t^3\).  
4. Collect like terms: constant term: \(a_1 - 2a_3\), \(t\) term: \(2a_2 - 12a_4\), \(t^2\) term: \(4a_3\), \(t^3\) term: \(8a_4\).  
5. Set up the system by matching coefficients with \(p(t)\):     \(a_1 - 2a_3 = 7\)     \(2a_2 - 12a_4 = -12\)     \(4a_3 = -8\)    \(8a_4 = 12\)  
6. Solve \(4a_3 = -8\) to get \(a_3 = -2\); verify by substitution: \(4(-2) = -8\) is correct.  
7. Solve \(8a_4 = 12\) to get \(a_4 = \frac{12}{8} = \frac{3}{2}\); verify: \(8 \times \frac{3}{2} = 12\) is correct.  
8. Substitute \(a_3 = -2\) into \(a_1 - 2a_3 = 7\): \(a_1 - 2(-2) = 7 \implies a_1 + 4 = 7 \implies a_1 = 3\); verify: \(3 - 2(-2) = 3 + 4 = 7\) is correct.  
9. Substitute \(a_4 = \frac{3}{2}\) into \(2a_2 - 12a_4 = -12\): \(2a_2 - 12 \times \frac{3}{2} = -12 \implies 2a_2 - 18 = -12 \implies 2a_2 = 6 \implies a_2 = 3\); verify: \(2 \times 3 - 18 = 6 - 18 = -12\) is correct.  
10. The coordinate vector of \(p\) relative to \(\mathcal{B}\) is \(\begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{bmatrix}\).
