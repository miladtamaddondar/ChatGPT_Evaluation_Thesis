1. Write the augmented matrix for the system $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}$: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\ -2 & 1 & 6 & 7 \\ 3 & -2 & -5 & -3\end{bmatrix}$.

2. Add $2$ times Row 1 to Row 2: $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 + 2R_1$: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 5 \\ 3 & -2 & -5 & -3\end{bmatrix}$.

3. Subtract $3$ times Row 1 from Row 3: $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 3R_1$: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 5 \\ 0 & -2 & 1 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.

4. Add $2$ times Row 2 to Row 3: $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 + 2R_2$: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 5 \\ 0 & 0 & 5 & 10\end{bmatrix}$.

5. Divide Row 3 by $5$: $R_3 \leftarrow \frac{1}{5}R_3$: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 5 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 2\end{bmatrix}$.

6. Subtract $2$ times Row 3 from Row 2: $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 2R_3$: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 2\end{bmatrix}$.

7. Add $2$ times Row 3 to Row 1: $R_1 \leftarrow R_1 + 2R_3$: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 2\end{bmatrix}$.

8. The solution is $\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}3 \\ 1 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$ and since the matrix is invertible (no free variables), the solution is unique.
