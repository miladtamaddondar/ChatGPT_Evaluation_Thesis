1. We are asked to find all $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^4$ such that $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$, i.e., solve the homogeneous system $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$ with $A = \begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4\end{bmatrix}$.
2. Let $\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4\end{bmatrix}$, so the system is: $1x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$, $x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0$, $2x_1 - 6x_2 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0$.
3. Write the augmented matrix: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.
4. Subtract $2$ times row 1 from row 3: row 3 becomes $[2 - 2\cdot1, -6 - 2\cdot(-4), 6 - 2\cdot7, -4 - 2\cdot(-5), 0] = [0, 2, -8, 6, 0]$.
5. The new matrix is $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -8 & 6 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.
6. Subtract $2$ times row 2 from row 3: row 3 becomes $[0, 2-2\cdot1, -8-2\cdot(-4), 6-2\cdot3, 0] = [0, 0, 0, 0, 0]$.
7. The reduced matrix is $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.
8. Write the equations: $x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$ and $x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0$.
9. Express $x_2$ in terms of $x_3$ and $x_4$: $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$.
10. Substitute $x_2$ into the first equation: $x_1 - 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.
11. Simplify: $x_1 - 16x_3 + 12x_4 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.
12. Combine like terms: $x_1 + (-16x_3 + 7x_3) + (12x_4 - 5x_4) = 0$ so $x_1 - 9x_3 + 7x_4 = 0$.
13. Solve for $x_1$: $x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4$.
14. The general solution is $x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4$, $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$, $x_3 = x_3$, $x_4 = x_4$.
15. Therefore, the solution set is $\mathbf{x} = x_3 \begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0\end{bmatrix} + x_4 \begin{bmatrix}-7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}$ for all $x_3, x_4 \in \mathbb{R}$.
