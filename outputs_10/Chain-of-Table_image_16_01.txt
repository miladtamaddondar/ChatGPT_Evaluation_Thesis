1. Find \( T(1) \): \( T(1) = T(1 + 0t + 0t^2) = 3 \cdot 1 + (5 \cdot 1 - 2 \cdot 0)t + (4 \cdot 0 + 0)t^2 = 3 + 5t \).  
2. Find \( T(t) \): \( T(t) = T(0 + 1t + 0t^2) = 3 \cdot 0 + (5 \cdot 0 - 2 \cdot 1)t + (4 \cdot 1 + 0)t^2 = -2t + 4t^2 \).  
3. Find \( T(t^2) \): \( T(t^2) = T(0 + 0t + 1t^2) = 3 \cdot 0 + (5 \cdot 0 - 2 \cdot 0)t + (4 \cdot 0 + 1)t^2 = t^2 \).  
4. Write each result as a coordinate vector relative to \( \mathcal{B} = \{1, t, t^2\} \): \( T(1) = 3 + 5t + 0t^2 \rightarrow \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 5 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \), \( T(t) = 0 + (-2)t + 4t^2 \rightarrow \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ -2 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} \), \( T(t^2) = 0 + 0t + 1t^2 \rightarrow \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \).  
5. Form the matrix by placing these vectors as columns: The matrix representation of \( T \) relative to \( \mathcal{B} \) is \( \begin{bmatrix} 3 & 0 & 0 \\ 5 & -2 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \).
