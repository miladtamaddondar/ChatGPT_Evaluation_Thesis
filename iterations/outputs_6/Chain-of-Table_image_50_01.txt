8. The orthogonal projection is \(5p_0 + 0p_1 = 5\).
9. For part (b), we seek \(q = p_2 - \mathrm{proj}_{V}(p_2) = t^2 - 5\), which is orthogonal to both \(p_0\) and \(p_1\).
