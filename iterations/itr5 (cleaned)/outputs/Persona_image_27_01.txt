1. The linear transformation \( T \) is determined by \( T(\mathbf{e}_1) = \mathbf{y}_1 \) and \( T(\mathbf{e}_2) = \mathbf{y}_2 \), so the standard matrix of \( T \) is \( A = [\mathbf{y}_1 \ \mathbf{y}_2] = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \).
2. To find the image of \( \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} \), compute \( A \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} \).
3. Multiply: \( 2 \times 5 + (-1) \times (-3) = 10 + 3 = 13 \) and \( 5 \times 5 + 6 \times (-3) = 25 - 18 = 7 \), so the image is \( \begin{bmatrix} 13 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} \).
4. To find the image of \( \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} \), compute \( A \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} \).
5. Multiply: \( 2x_1 - x_2 \) and \( 5x_1 + 6x_2 \), so the image is \( \begin{bmatrix} 2x_1 - x_2 \\ 5x_1 + 6x_2 \end{bmatrix} \).
