a. $\left(\dfrac{\mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v}}{\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{v}}\right) \mathbf{v}$
1. Compute $\mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v}$ using the dot product formula: $(-1)(4) + (2)(6) = -4 + 12 = 8$.
2. Compute $\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{v}$ using the dot product formula: $(4)(4) + (6)(6) = 16 + 36 = 52$.
3. Compute the scalar $\dfrac{\mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v}}{\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{v}} = \dfrac{8}{52} = \dfrac{2}{13}$.
4. Multiply the scalar by vector $\mathbf{v}$: $\dfrac{2}{13} \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \dfrac{8}{13} \\ \dfrac{12}{13} \end{bmatrix}$.
5. Final answer for (a): $\left(\dfrac{\mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v}}{\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{v}}\right) \mathbf{v} = \begin{bmatrix} \dfrac{8}{13} \\ \dfrac{12}{13} \end{bmatrix}$.
b. $\left(\dfrac{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{w}}{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{x}}\right) \mathbf{x}$
1. Compute $\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{w}$ using the dot product formula: $(6)(3) + (-2)(-1) + (3)(-5) = 18 + 2 - 15 = 5$.
2. Compute $\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{x}$ using the dot product formula: $(6)^2 + (-2)^2 + (3)^2 = 36 + 4 + 9 = 49$.
3. Compute the scalar $\dfrac{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{w}}{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{x}} = \dfrac{5}{49}$.
4. Multiply the scalar by vector $\mathbf{x}$: $\dfrac{5}{49} \begin{bmatrix} 6 \\ -2 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \dfrac{30}{49} \\ -\dfrac{10}{49} \\ \dfrac{15}{49} \end{bmatrix}$.
5. Final answer for (b): $\left(\dfrac{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{w}}{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{x}}\right) \mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix} \dfrac{30}{49} \\ -\dfrac{10}{49} \\ \dfrac{15}{49} \end{bmatrix}$.
