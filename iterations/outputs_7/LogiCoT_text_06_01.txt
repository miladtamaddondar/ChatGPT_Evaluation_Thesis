4. Solving \( -x_1 + 2x_2 = 0 \) gives \( x_1 = 2x_2 \); let \( x_2 = 1 \), then \( x_1 = 2 \), so one solution is \( x^{(1)} = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} \).
