1. We are asked to find all $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^4$ such that $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$, i.e., to solve the homogeneous system $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$, where $A = \begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4\end{bmatrix}$.
2. Let $\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4\end{bmatrix}$; then the system is: $1x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$, $0x_1 + 1x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0$, $2x_1 - 6x_2 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0$.
3. The second equation gives $x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0$, so $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$; this is correct because we can isolate $x_2$ directly.
4. Substitute $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$ into the first equation: $x_1 - 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.
5. Simplify: $x_1 - 16x_3 + 12x_4 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0 \implies x_1 - 9x_3 + 7x_4 = 0$; this is correct by combining like terms.
6. Thus, $x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4$; this follows from isolating $x_1$.
7. Substitute $x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4$ and $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$ into the third equation: $2(9x_3 - 7x_4) - 6(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0$.
8. Expand: $18x_3 - 14x_4 - 24x_3 + 18x_4 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0$.
9. Combine like terms: $(18x_3 - 24x_3 + 6x_3) + (-14x_4 + 18x_4 - 4x_4) = (0x_3) + (0x_4) = 0$; this confirms the third equation is always satisfied for any $x_3, x_4$.
10. Therefore, the general solution is $\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}9x_3 - 7x_4 \\ 4x_3 - 3x_4 \\ x_3 \\ x_4\end{bmatrix}$ for all $x_3, x_4 \in \mathbb{R}$.
11. Final answer: All $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^4$ mapped to zero by $A$ are $\boxed{\mathbf{x} = x_3\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0\end{bmatrix} + x_4\begin{bmatrix}-7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1\end{bmatrix},\ \text{for all } x_3, x_4 \in \mathbb{R}}$.
