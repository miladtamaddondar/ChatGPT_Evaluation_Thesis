1. Write the basis as \( B = \{1,\, 2t,\, -2 + 4t^2,\, -12t + 8t^3\} \) and express \( p(t) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3 \) as a linear combination: \( p(t) = a(1) + b(2t) + c(-2 + 4t^2) + d(-12t + 8t^3) \).
2. Expand and collect like terms: \( p(t) = a + 2b t + (-2c) + 4c t^2 + (-12d t) + 8d t^3 \Rightarrow (a - 2c) + (2b - 12d)t + 4c t^2 + 8d t^3 \).
3. Set up the system by matching coefficients: \( a - 2c = 7 \), \( 2b - 12d = -12 \), \( 4c = -8 \), \( 8d = 12 \).
4. Write the augmented matrix: \( \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & -12 \\ 0 & 0 & 4 & 0 & -8 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 8 & 12 \end{array}\right] \).
5. Row 3: Divide by 4 to make leading 1: \( R_3 \to \frac{1}{4}R_3 \Rightarrow \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & -12 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 8 & 12 \end{array}\right] \).
6. Row 4: Divide by 8 to make leading 1: \( R_4 \to \frac{1}{8}R_4 \Rightarrow \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & -12 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right] \).
7. Row 2: Eliminate -12 in column 4 using row 4: \( R_2 \to R_2 + 12R_4 \Rightarrow \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & 0 & 6 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right] \).
8. Row 2: Divide by 2 to make leading 1: \( R_2 \to \frac{1}{2}R_2 \Rightarrow \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right] \).
9. Row 1: Eliminate -2 in column 3 using row 3: \( R_1 \to R_1 + 2R_3 \Rightarrow \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right] \).
