1. The basis \(\mathcal{B}\) is \(\{1,\, 2t,\, -2+4t^2,\, -12t+8t^3\}\).  
2. Write \(p(t) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3\) as a linear combination: \(p(t) = a\cdot1 + b\cdot2t + c(-2+4t^2) + d(-12t+8t^3)\).  
3. Expand: \(a + 2b t + (-2c + 4c t^2) + (-12d t + 8d t^3) = a + 2b t - 2c + 4c t^2 - 12d t + 8d t^3\).  
4. Collect like terms: constant: \(a-2c\), \(t\): \(2b-12d\), \(t^2\): \(4c\), \(t^3\): \(8d\).  
5. Set up equations by matching coefficients with \(p(t)\): \(a-2c=7\), \(2b-12d=-12\), \(4c=-8\), \(8d=12\).  
6. Solve \(4c=-8 \implies c=-2\).  
7. Solve \(8d=12 \implies d=\frac{3}{2}\).  
8. Substitute \(c=-2\) into \(a-2c=7\): \(a-2(-2)=7 \implies a+4=7 \implies a=3\).  
9. Substitute \(d=\frac{3}{2}\) into \(2b-12d=-12\): \(2b-12(\frac{3}{2})=-12 \implies 2b-18=-12 \implies 2b=6 \implies b=3\).  
10. The coordinate vector of \(p\) relative to \(\mathcal{B}\) is \(\begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{bmatrix}\).
