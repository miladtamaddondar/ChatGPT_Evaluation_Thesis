1. Set up the equation $a_1(1) + a_2(2t) + a_3(-2 + 4t^2) + a_4(-12t + 8t^3) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3$ and expand: $a_1 + 2a_2 t + a_3(-2) + a_3(4t^2) + a_4(-12t) + a_4(8t^3) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3$.

2. Collect like terms: $(a_1 - 2a_3) + (2a_2 - 12a_4)t + 4a_3 t^2 + 8a_4 t^3 = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3$.

3. Set up the system by equating coefficients: $a_1 - 2a_3 = 7$, $2a_2 - 12a_4 = -12$, $4a_3 = -8$, $8a_4 = 12$.

4. Write the augmented matrix:
\[
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & | & 7 \\
0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & | & -12 \\
0 & 0 & 4 & 0 & | & -8 \\
0 & 0 & 0 & 8 & | & 12 \\
\end{bmatrix}
\]

5. Row 3: $4a_3 = -8 \implies a_3 = -2$; Row 4: $8a_4 = 12 \implies a_4 = \frac{3}{2}$.

6. Substitute $a_3 = -2$ into Row 1: $a_1 - 2(-2) = 7 \implies a_1 + 4 = 7 \implies a_1 = 3$.

7. Substitute $a_4 = \frac{3}{2}$ into Row 2: $2a_2 - 12 \cdot \frac{3}{2} = -12 \implies 2a_2 - 18 = -12 \implies 2a_2 = 6 \implies a_2 = 3$.

8. The coordinate vector is $\begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{bmatrix}_{\mathcal{B}}$.
