1. To find the inverse of a 2x2 matrix \(\begin{bmatrix}a & b \\ c & d\end{bmatrix}\), use the formula \(\frac{1}{ad-bc}\begin{bmatrix}d & -b \\ -c & a\end{bmatrix}\). 
2. For the matrix \(\begin{bmatrix}8 & 6 \\ 5 & 4\end{bmatrix}\), compute the determinant: \(ad-bc = (8)(4) - (6)(5) = 32 - 30 = 2\). 
3. The inverse is \(\frac{1}{2}\begin{bmatrix}4 & -6 \\ -5 & 8\end{bmatrix}\). 
4. Therefore, the inverse is \(\begin{bmatrix}2 & -3 \\ -2.5 & 4\end{bmatrix}\).
