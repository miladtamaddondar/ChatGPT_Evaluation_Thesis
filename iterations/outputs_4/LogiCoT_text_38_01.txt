5. Solve $4a_3 = -8$ to get $a_3 = -2$; verify: $4(-2) = -8$ is correct.  
6. Solve $8a_4 = 12$ to get $a_4 = \frac{3}{2}$; verify: $8 \cdot \frac{3}{2} = 12$ is correct.
