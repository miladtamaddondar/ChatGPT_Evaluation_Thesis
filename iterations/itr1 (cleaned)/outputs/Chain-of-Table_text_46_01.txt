1. Write the normal equations $A^T A \mathbf{x} = A^T \mathbf{b}$: $A^T A = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 2 & 2 \\ 2 & 2 & 0 \\ 2 & 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix}$ and $A^T \mathbf{b} = \begin{bmatrix} 14 \\ 4 \\ 10 \end{bmatrix}$, so the augmented matrix is $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 4 & 2 & 2 & 14 \\ 2 & 2 & 0 & 4 \\ 2 & 0 & 2 & 10 \end{array}\right]$.
2. Divide row 1 by 4: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0.5 & 0.5 & 3.5 \\ 2 & 2 & 0 & 4 \\ 2 & 0 & 2 & 10 \end{array}\right]$.
3. Subtract 2 times row 1 from row 2: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0.5 & 0.5 & 3.5 \\ 0 & 1 & -1 & -3 \\ 2 & 0 & 2 & 10 \end{array}\right]$.
4. Subtract 2 times row 1 from row 3: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0.5 & 0.5 & 3.5 \\ 0 & 1 & -1 & -3 \\ 0 & -1 & 1 & 3 \end{array}\right]$.
5. Add row 2 to row 3: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0.5 & 0.5 & 3.5 \\ 0 & 1 & -1 & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]$.
6. The system reduces to $x_1 + 0.5x_2 + 0.5x_3 = 3.5$ and $x_2 - x_3 = -3$, so $x_2 = -3 + x_3$ and $x_1 = 3.5 - 0.5x_2 - 0.5x_3$.
7. Substitute $x_2$ into $x_1$: $x_1 = 3.5 - 0.5(-3 + x_3) - 0.5x_3 = 3.5 + 1.5 - 0.5x_3 - 0.5x_3 = 5 - x_3$.
8. The least-squares solutions are $\boxed{\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix} 5 - x_3 \\ -3 + x_3 \\ x_3 \end{bmatrix}, \text{ for all } x_3 \in \mathbb{R}}$.
