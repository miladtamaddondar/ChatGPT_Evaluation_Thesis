1. Let the columns of the matrix be \( \mathbf{a}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \), \( \mathbf{a}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \), and \( \mathbf{a}_3 = \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ 7 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \).  
2. Set \( \mathbf{v}_1 = \mathbf{a}_1 \) as the first vector in the orthogonal basis.  
3. Compute the projection of \( \mathbf{a}_2 \) onto \( \mathbf{v}_1 \): \( \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{v}_1} \mathbf{a}_2 = \frac{\mathbf{a}_2 \cdot \mathbf{v}_1}{\mathbf{v}_1 \cdot \mathbf{v}_1} \mathbf{v}_1 = \frac{2(1) + 1(-1) + 4(-1) + (-4)(1) + 2(1)}{1^2 + (-1)^2 + (-1)^2 + 1^2 + 1^2} \mathbf{v}_1 = \frac{2 - 1 - 4 - 4 + 2}{5} \mathbf{v}_1 = \frac{-5}{5} \mathbf{v}_1 = -\mathbf{v}_1 \).  
4. Subtract the projection from \( \mathbf{a}_2 \) to get \( \mathbf{v}_2 \): \( \mathbf{v}_2 = \mathbf{a}_2 - \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{v}_1} \mathbf{a}_2 = \mathbf{a}_2 - (-\mathbf{v}_1) = \mathbf{a}_2 + \mathbf{v}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix} \).  
5. Compute the projection of \( \mathbf{a}_3 \) onto \( \mathbf{v}_1 \): \( \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{v}_1} \mathbf{a}_3 = \frac{\mathbf{a}_3 \cdot \mathbf{v}_1}{\mathbf{v}_1 \cdot \mathbf{v}_1} \mathbf{v}_1 = \frac{5(1) + (-4)(-1) + (-3)(-1) + 7(1) + 1(1)}{5} \mathbf{v}_1 = \frac{5 + 4 + 3 + 7 + 1}{5} \mathbf{v}_1 = \frac{20}{5} \mathbf{v}_1 = 4\mathbf{v}_1 \).  
6. Compute the projection of \( \mathbf{a}_3 \) onto \( \mathbf{v}_2 \): \( \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{v}_2} \mathbf{a}_3 = \frac{\mathbf{a}_3 \cdot \mathbf{v}_2}{\mathbf{v}_2 \cdot \mathbf{v}_2} \mathbf{v}_2 = \frac{5(3) + (-4)(0) + (-3)(3) + 7(-3) + 1(3)}{3^2 + 0^2 + 3^2 + (-3)^2 + 3^2} \mathbf{v}_2 = \frac{15 + 0 - 9 - 21 + 3}{9 + 0 + 9 + 9 + 9} \mathbf{v}_2 = \frac{-12}{36} \mathbf{v}_2 = -\frac{1}{3} \mathbf{v}_2 \).  
7. Subtract both projections from \( \mathbf{a}_3 \) to get \( \mathbf{v}_3 \): \( \mathbf{v}_3 = \mathbf{a}_3 - 4\mathbf{v}_1 - \left(-\frac{1}{3}\mathbf{v}_2\right) = \mathbf{a}_3 - 4\mathbf{v}_1 + \frac{1}{3}\mathbf{v}_2 \).  
8. Compute \( \mathbf{a}_3 - 4\mathbf{v}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ 7 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} - 4\begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 3 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} \).  
9. Add \( \frac{1}{3}\mathbf{v}_2 \) to get \( \mathbf{v}_3 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 3 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} + \frac{1}{3}\begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1+1 \\ 0+0 \\ 1+1 \\ 3+(-1) \\ -3+1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ -2 \end{bmatrix} \).  
10. The orthogonal basis for the column space is \( \left\{ \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ -2 \end{bmatrix} \right\} \).
