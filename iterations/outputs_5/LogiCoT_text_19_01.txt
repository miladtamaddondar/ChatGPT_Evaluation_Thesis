The $\mathcal{B}$-matrix for $T$ is the matrix representation $[T]_{\mathcal{B}}$ such that $[T(\mathbf{x})]_{\mathcal{B}} = [T]_{\mathcal{B}}[\mathbf{x}]_{\mathcal{B}}$ for all $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^2$; to find $[T]_{\mathcal{B}}$, we compute $[T(\mathbf{b}_1)]_{\mathcal{B}}$ and $[T(\mathbf{b}_2)]_{\mathcal{B}}$ and use them as columns.  
2. Compute $T(\mathbf{b}_1) = A\mathbf{b}_1 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$; this follows by matrix multiplication: $1\cdot1 + 1\cdot1 = 2$, $-1\cdot1 + 3\cdot1 = 2$.  
3. Express $\begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$ as a linear combination of $\mathbf{b}_1$ and $\mathbf{b}_2$: set $\begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix} = c_1\begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} + c_2\begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix}$, so $2 = c_1 + 5c_2$ and $2 = c_1 + 4c_2$.  
4. Subtract the second equation from the first: $(c_1 + 5c_2) - (c_1 + 4c_2) = 2 - 2 \implies c_2 = 0$; then $c_1 = 2$.  
5. Therefore, $[T(\mathbf{b}_1)]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 0\end{bmatrix}$, since $T(\mathbf{b}_1) = 2\mathbf{b}_1 + 0\mathbf{b}_2$.  
6. Compute $T(\mathbf{b}_2) = A\mathbf{b}_2 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix}$; check: $1\cdot5 + 1\cdot4 = 9$, $-1\cdot5 + 3\cdot4 = -5 + 12 = 7$.  
7. Express $\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix}$ as a linear combination of $\mathbf{b}_1$ and $\mathbf{b}_2$: set $\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix} = d_1\begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} + d_2\begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix}$, so $9 = d_1 + 5d_2$ and $7 = d_1 + 4d_2$.  
8. Subtract the second equation from the first: $(d_1 + 5d_2) - (d_1 + 4d_2) = 9 - 7 \implies d_2 = 2$; then $d_1 = 7 - 4\cdot2 = 7 - 8 = -1$.  
9. Therefore, $[T(\mathbf{b}_2)]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix}-1 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$, since $T(\mathbf{b}_2) = -1\mathbf{b}_1 + 2\mathbf{b}_2$.  
10. The $\mathcal{B}$-matrix for $T$ is $[T]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix}2 & -1 \\ 0 & 2\end{bmatrix}$, with columns $[T(\mathbf{b}_1)]_{\mathcal{B}}$ and $[T(\mathbf{b}_2)]_{\mathcal{B}}$.
