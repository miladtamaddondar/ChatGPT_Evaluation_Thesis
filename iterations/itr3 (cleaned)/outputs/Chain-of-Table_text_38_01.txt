1. Write $p(t)$ as a linear combination of the Hermite polynomials: $p(t) = a_1 \cdot 1 + a_2 \cdot 2t + a_3 \cdot (-2 + 4t^2) + a_4 \cdot (-12t + 8t^3)$.  
2. Expand and collect like terms: $p(t) = a_1 + 2a_2 t - 2a_3 + 4a_3 t^2 - 12a_4 t + 8a_4 t^3 = (a_1 - 2a_3) + (2a_2 - 12a_4)t + 4a_3 t^2 + 8a_4 t^3$.  
3. Set up the system by matching coefficients with $p(t) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3$:  $\begin{cases} a_1 - 2a_3 = 7 \\ 2a_2 - 12a_4 = -12 \\ 4a_3 = -8 \\ 8a_4 = 12 \end{cases}$  
4. Write the augmented matrix:  \[\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & | & 7 \\0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & | & -12 \\0 & 0 & 4 & 0 & | & -8 \\0 & 0 & 0 & 8 & | & 12\end{bmatrix}\]  
5. Row 3: $4a_3 = -8 \implies a_3 = -2$; Row 4: $8a_4 = 12 \implies a_4 = \frac{3}{2}$.  
6. Substitute $a_4 = \frac{3}{2}$ into Row 2: $2a_2 - 12 \cdot \frac{3}{2} = -12 \implies 2a_2 - 18 = -12 \implies 2a_2 = 6 \implies a_2 = 3$.  
7. Substitute $a_3 = -2$ into Row 1: $a_1 - 2(-2) = 7 \implies a_1 + 4 = 7 \implies a_1 = 3$.  
8. The coordinate vector of $p$ relative to $\mathcal{B}$ is $\boxed{\begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{bmatrix}}$.
