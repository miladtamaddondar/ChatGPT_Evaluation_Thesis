1. Let $\mathcal{B} = \{1, 2t, -2 + 4t^2, -12t + 8t^3\}$ and write $p(t) = a_1 \cdot 1 + a_2 \cdot 2t + a_3(-2 + 4t^2) + a_4(-12t + 8t^3)$; we seek $[p]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix} a_1 \\ a_2 \\ a_3 \\ a_4 \end{bmatrix}$.
2. Expand the right-hand side: $a_1 + 2a_2 t + a_3(-2 + 4t^2) + a_4(-12t + 8t^3) = a_1 - 2a_3 + 2a_2 t - 12a_4 t + 4a_3 t^2 + 8a_4 t^3$.
3. Collect like terms: constant term $a_1 - 2a_3$, $t$ term $2a_2 - 12a_4$, $t^2$ term $4a_3$, $t^3$ term $8a_4$; thus $p(t) = (a_1 - 2a_3) + (2a_2 - 12a_4)t + 4a_3 t^2 + 8a_4 t^3$.
4. Set up the system by equating coefficients with $p(t) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3$: $a_1 - 2a_3 = 7$, $2a_2 - 12a_4 = -12$, $4a_3 = -8$, $8a_4 = 12$.
5. Solve $4a_3 = -8 \implies a_3 = -2$; verify: $4 \cdot (-2) = -8$ is correct.
6. Solve $8a_4 = 12 \implies a_4 = \frac{12}{8} = \frac{3}{2}$; verify: $8 \cdot \frac{3}{2} = 12$ is correct.
7. Substitute $a_3 = -2$ into $a_1 - 2a_3 = 7 \implies a_1 - 2(-2) = 7 \implies a_1 + 4 = 7 \implies a_1 = 3$; verify: $3 - 2(-2) = 3 + 4 = 7$ is correct.
8. Substitute $a_4 = \frac{3}{2}$ into $2a_2 - 12a_4 = -12 \implies 2a_2 - 12 \cdot \frac{3}{2} = -12 \implies 2a_2 - 18 = -12 \implies 2a_2 = 6 \implies a_2 = 3$; verify: $2 \cdot 3 - 12 \cdot \frac{3}{2} = 6 - 18 = -12$ is correct.
9. The coordinate vector of $p$ relative to $\mathcal{B}$ is $\boxed{\begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{bmatrix}}$.
