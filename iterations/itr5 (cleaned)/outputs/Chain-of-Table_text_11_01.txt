1. To find the eigenvalues, set up the characteristic equation by subtracting \(\lambda\) from the diagonal: \(\begin{bmatrix}0-\lambda & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 2-\lambda & 5 \\ 0 & 0 & 1-\lambda\end{bmatrix}\).
2. The determinant is \((0-\lambda)[(2-\lambda)(1-\lambda)-0] - 0 + 0 = -\lambda(2-\lambda)(1-\lambda)\).
3. Set the determinant to zero: \(-\lambda(2-\lambda)(1-\lambda) = 0\).
4. The eigenvalues are \(\boxed{0, 1, 2}\).
