Write the equation $q = c_1 p_1 + c_2 p_2 + c_3 p_3$ with $[q]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix}$, so $q = -1\cdot p_1 + 1\cdot p_2 + 2\cdot p_3$; set up the augmented matrix for coefficients of $1$, $t$, $t^2$: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 1 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -3 & -3 & 2 \end{array}\right]$.
2. Substitute $p_1 = 1 + t^2$, $p_2 = t - 3t^2$, $p_3 = 1 + t - 3t^2$ into $q$ and collect like terms: $q = -1(1 + t^2) + 1(t - 3t^2) + 2(1 + t - 3t^2) = (-1 + 2) + (1 + 2)t + (-1 - 3 + -6)t^2 = 1 + 3t - 10t^2$.
3. Therefore, the polynomial $q$ is $q = 1 + 3t - 10t^2$.
