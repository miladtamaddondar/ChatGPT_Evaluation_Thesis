1. Find the eigenvalues of \(A\) by solving \(\det(A - \lambda I) = 0\): \(\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}0.6-\lambda & 0.3 \\ 0.4 & 0.7-\lambda\end{bmatrix}\right) = (0.6-\lambda)(0.7-\lambda) - (0.4)(0.3) = 0\).

2. Expand and simplify the characteristic equation: \((0.6-\lambda)(0.7-\lambda) - 0.12 = 0 \implies 0.42 - 0.6\lambda - 0.7\lambda + \lambda^2 - 0.12 = 0 \implies \lambda^2 - 1.3\lambda + 0.3 = 0\).

3. Solve the quadratic equation for \(\lambda\): \(\lambda = \frac{1.3 \pm \sqrt{(1.3)^2 - 4 \cdot 0.3}}{2} = \frac{1.3 \pm \sqrt{1.69 - 1.2}}{2} = \frac{1.3 \pm \sqrt{0.49}}{2} = \frac{1.3 \pm 0.7}{2}\).

4. The eigenvalues are \(\lambda_1 = 1\) and \(\lambda_2 = 0.3\).

5. Verify that \(\mathbf{v}_1 = \begin{bmatrix}3/7 \\ 4/7\end{bmatrix}\) is an eigenvector for \(\lambda_1 = 1\) by computing \(A\mathbf{v}_1\): \(A\mathbf{v}_1 = \begin{bmatrix}0.6 & 0.3 \\ 0.4 & 0.7\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}3/7 \\ 4/7\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}0.6 \cdot 3/7 + 0.3 \cdot 4/7 \\ 0.4 \cdot 3/7 + 0.7 \cdot 4/7\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}(1.8 + 1.2)/7 \\ (1.2 + 2.8)/7\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}3/7 \\ 4/7\end{bmatrix} = \mathbf{v}_1\).

6. Find an eigenvector \(\mathbf{v}_2\) for \(\lambda_2 = 0.3\) by solving \((A - 0.3I)\mathbf{v}_2 = 0\): \(A - 0.3I = \begin{bmatrix}0.3 & 0.3 \\ 0.4 & 0.4\end{bmatrix}\), so \(\begin{bmatrix}0.3 & 0.3 \\ 0.4 & 0.4\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}x \\ y\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}0 \\ 0\end{bmatrix}\).

7. Write the system: \(0.3x + 0.3y = 0\) and \(0.4x + 0.4y = 0\), which reduces to \(x + y = 0\), so \(y = -x\).

8. Choose \(x = 1\), then \(y = -1\), so \(\mathbf{v}_2 = \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ -1\end{bmatrix}\).

9. Verify linear independence of \(\mathbf{v}_1\) and \(\mathbf{v}_2\) by checking if one is a scalar multiple of the other: \(\mathbf{v}_1 = \begin{bmatrix}3/7 \\ 4/7\end{bmatrix}\) and \(\mathbf{v}_2 = \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ -1\end{bmatrix}\) are not scalar multiples, so they are linearly independent.

10. The basis for \(\mathbb{R}^2\) consisting of eigenvectors of \(A\) is \(\left\{\begin{bmatrix}3/7 \\ 4/7\end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ -1\end{bmatrix}\right\}\).
