4. Solve $(A^T A)\mathbf{x} = A^T \mathbf{b}$, i.e., $\begin{bmatrix} 14 & 2 \\ 2 & 42 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix}$.
5. Write the system: $14x_1 + 2x_2 = 4$ and $2x_1 + 42x_2 = 6$.
6. Solve the second equation for $x_1$: $2x_1 = 6 - 42x_2 \implies x_1 = 3 - 21x_2$.
