The standard basis vectors for \(\mathbb{R}^2\) are \(\mathbf{e}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}\) and \(\mathbf{e}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\), and the transformation \(T\) maps \(\mathbf{e}_1\) to \(\mathbf{y}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix}\) and \(\mathbf{e}_2\) to \(\mathbf{y}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix}\). 2. Since \(T\) is linear, its matrix \(A\) in the standard basis has columns \(\mathbf{y}_1\) and \(\mathbf{y}_2\), so \(A = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix}\). 3. To find \(T\left(\begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix}\right)\), use linearity: \(T\left(\begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix}\right) = 5T(\mathbf{e}_1) + (-3)T(\mathbf{e}_2) = 5\begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} - 3\begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 10 \\ 25 \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ -18 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 13 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix}\). 4. To verify, multiply \(A\) by \(\begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix}\): \(\begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \times 5 + (-1) \times (-3) \\ 5 \times 5 + 6 \times (-3) \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 10 + 3 \\ 25 - 18 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 13 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix}\), confirming the previous result. 5. For a general vector \(\begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix}\), \(T\left(\begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix}\right) = x_1 T(\mathbf{e}_1) + x_2 T(\mathbf{e}_2) = x_1 \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} + x_2 \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2x_1 - x_2 \\ 5x_1 + 6x_2 \end{bmatrix}\). 6. To verify, multiply \(A\) by \(\begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix}\): \(\begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2x_1 - x_2 \\ 5x_1 + 6x_2 \end{bmatrix}\), confirming the formula. 7. Final answers: \(T\left(\begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix}\right) = \begin{bmatrix} 13 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix}\) and \(T\left(\begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix}\right) = \begin{bmatrix} 2x_1 - x_2 \\ 5x_1 + 6x_2 \end{bmatrix}\).
