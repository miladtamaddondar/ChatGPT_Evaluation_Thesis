1. Write $T(\mathbf{x}) = x_1\mathbf{v}_1 + x_2\mathbf{v}_2 = x_1\begin{bmatrix} -2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} + x_2\begin{bmatrix} 7 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -2x_1 + 7x_2 \\ 5x_1 - 3x_2 \end{bmatrix}$.
2. Express as a matrix multiplication: $\begin{bmatrix} -2x_1 + 7x_2 \\ 5x_1 - 3x_2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -2 & 7 \\ 5 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix}$.
3. Therefore, the matrix $A$ such that $T(\mathbf{x}) = A\mathbf{x}$ is $A = \begin{bmatrix} -2 & 7 \\ 5 & -3 \end{bmatrix}$.
