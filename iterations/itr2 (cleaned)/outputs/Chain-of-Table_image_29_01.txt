1. Write the system of equations from \( T(x_1, x_2) = (3, 8) \): \( x_1 + x_2 = 3 \), \( 4x_1 + 5x_2 = 8 \).  
2. Write the augmented matrix: \( \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 4 & 5 & | & 8 \end{bmatrix} \).  
3. Row 2 minus 4 times Row 1: \( R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 4R_1 \): \( \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & | & -4 \end{bmatrix} \).  
4. Row 1 minus Row 2: \( R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - R_2 \): \( \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & | & 7 \\ 0 & 1 & | & -4 \end{bmatrix} \).  
5. Solution: \( x_1 = 7, x_2 = -4 \).
