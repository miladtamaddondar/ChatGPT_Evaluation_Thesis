1. To find the image of \( p(t) = 5 + 3t \) under \( T \), evaluate \( p(-1) = 5 + 3(-1) = 2 \), \( p(0) = 5 + 3(0) = 5 \), and \( p(1) = 5 + 3(1) = 8 \).
2. Thus, \( T(p) = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \\ 8 \end{bmatrix} \).
3. To find the matrix for \( T \), first apply \( T \) to the basis vectors: \( T(1) = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \), \( T(t) = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \), \( T(t^2) = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \).
4. The matrix for \( T \) relative to the given bases is \( \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -1 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \).
