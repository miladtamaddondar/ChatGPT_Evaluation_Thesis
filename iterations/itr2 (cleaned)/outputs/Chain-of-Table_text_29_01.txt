2. Replace row 2 with row 2 minus 4 times row 1: \(R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 4R_1\): \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & | & -4 \end{bmatrix}\).
3. Replace row 1 with row 1 minus row 2: \(R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - R_2\): \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & | & 7 \\ 0 & 1 & | & -4 \end{bmatrix}\).
