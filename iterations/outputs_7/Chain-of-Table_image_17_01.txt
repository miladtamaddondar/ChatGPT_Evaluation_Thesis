1. To find the image under \( T \) of \( p(t) = 5 + 3t \), evaluate at \( t = -1, 0, 1 \): \( p(-1) = 5 + 3(-1) = 2 \), \( p(0) = 5 + 3(0) = 5 \), \( p(1) = 5 + 3(1) = 8 \), so \( T(5 + 3t) = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \\ 8 \end{bmatrix} \).

To find the matrix for \( T \) relative to the basis \( \{1, t, t^2\} \) for \( \mathbb{P}_2 \) and the standard basis for \( \mathbb{R}^3 \), compute \( T(1) = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \), \( T(t) = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \), \( T(t^2) = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \), so the matrix is \( \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -1 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \).
