1. Form the augmented matrix for $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}_1$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3 & 1 \end{array}\right]$.
2. $R_2 \to R_2 + R_1$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 4 & 2 \end{array}\right]$.
3. $R_2 \to \frac{1}{4}R_2$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & \frac{1}{2} \end{array}\right]$.
4. $R_1 \to R_1 - R_2$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 0 & \frac{1}{2} \\ 0 & 1 & \frac{1}{2} \end{array}\right]$.
5. So $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}_1$ has solution $\mathbf{x}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{1}{2} \\ \frac{1}{2} \end{bmatrix}$.
6. Form the augmented matrix for $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}_2$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 1 & 5 \\ -1 & 3 & 4 \end{array}\right]$.
7. $R_2 \to R_2 + R_1$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 1 & 5 \\ 0 & 4 & 9 \end{array}\right]$.
8. $R_2 \to \frac{1}{4}R_2$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 1 & 5 \\ 0 & 1 & \frac{9}{4} \end{array}\right]$.
9. $R_1 \to R_1 - R_2$: $\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 0 & \frac{11}{4} \\ 0 & 1 & \frac{9}{4} \end{array}\right]$.
10. So $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}_2$ has solution $\mathbf{x}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{11}{4} \\ \frac{9}{4} \end{bmatrix}$.
11. The $\mathcal{B}$-matrix for $T$ is $\boxed{\begin{bmatrix} \frac{1}{2} & \frac{11}{4} \\ \frac{1}{2} & \frac{9}{4} \end{bmatrix}}$.
