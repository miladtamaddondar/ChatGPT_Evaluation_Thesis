1. We are given \( T(\mathbf{x}) = x_1 \mathbf{v}_1 + x_2 \mathbf{v}_2 \) where \( \mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} \), \( \mathbf{v}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} -2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} \), and \( \mathbf{v}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 7 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} \). We want \( T(\mathbf{x}) = A\mathbf{x} \).
2. Write \( T(\mathbf{x}) = x_1 \begin{bmatrix} -2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} + x_2 \begin{bmatrix} 7 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -2x_1 + 7x_2 \\ 5x_1 - 3x_2 \end{bmatrix} \).
3. Express as a matrix multiplication: \( \begin{bmatrix} -2x_1 + 7x_2 \\ 5x_1 - 3x_2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -2 & 7 \\ 5 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} \).
4. Therefore, the matrix \( A \) is \( \boxed{A = \begin{bmatrix} -2 & 7 \\ 5 & -3 \end{bmatrix}} \).
