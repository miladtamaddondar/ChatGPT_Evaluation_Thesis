1. Write the augmented matrix for the system $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}$: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\ -2 & 1 & 6 & 7 \\ 3 & -2 & -5 & -3\end{bmatrix}$.
2. Add $2$ times row $1$ to row $2$: $R_2 \to R_2 + 2R_1$, $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 5 \\ 3 & -2 & -5 & -3\end{bmatrix}$.
3. Subtract $3$ times row $1$ from row $3$: $R_3 \to R_3 - 3R_1$, $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 5 \\ 0 & -2 & 1 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.
4. Add $2$ times row $2$ to row $3$: $R_3 \to R_3 + 2R_2$, $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 5 \\ 0 & 0 & 5 & 10\end{bmatrix}$.
5. Divide row $3$ by $5$: $R_3 \to \frac{1}{5}R_3$, $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 5 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 2\end{bmatrix}$.
6. Subtract $2$ times row $3$ from row $2$: $R_2 \to R_2 - 2R_3$, $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 2\end{bmatrix}$.
7. Add $2$ times row $3$ to row $1$: $R_1 \to R_1 + 2R_3$, $\begin{bmatrix}1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 2\end{bmatrix}$.
8. The solution is unique: $\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}3 \\ 1 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$.
