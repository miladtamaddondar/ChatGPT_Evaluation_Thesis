1. To find all $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^4$ mapped to the zero vector by $A$, we solve $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$, i.e., find the null space of $A$.
2. Write the system as augmented matrix: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.
3. Subtract $2$ times row 1 from row 3: Row 3 $\to$ Row 3 $- 2 \times$ Row 1 $\implies$ $[2 - 2 \cdot 1, -6 - 2 \cdot (-4), 6 - 2 \cdot 7, -4 - 2 \cdot (-5), 0 - 2 \cdot 0] = [0, 2, -8, 6, 0]$.
4. The new matrix is $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -8 & 6 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.
5. Subtract $2$ times row 2 from row 3: Row 3 $\to$ Row 3 $- 2 \times$ Row 2 $\implies$ $[0, 2 - 2 \cdot 1, -8 - 2 \cdot (-4), 6 - 2 \cdot 3, 0 - 2 \cdot 0] = [0, 0, 0, 0, 0]$.
6. The reduced matrix is $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.
7. Write the system of equations: $x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$, $x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0$.
8. Express $x_2$ in terms of $x_3$ and $x_4$: $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$.
9. Substitute $x_2$ into the first equation: $x_1 - 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.
10. Simplify: $x_1 - 16x_3 + 12x_4 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0 \implies x_1 - 9x_3 + 7x_4 = 0$.
11. Solve for $x_1$: $x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4$.
12. Let $x_3 = s$, $x_4 = t$ where $s, t \in \mathbb{R}$, then $x_1 = 9s - 7t$, $x_2 = 4s - 3t$, $x_3 = s$, $x_4 = t$.
13. The solution set is $\mathbf{x} = s \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + t \begin{bmatrix} -7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}$ for $s, t \in \mathbb{R}$.
