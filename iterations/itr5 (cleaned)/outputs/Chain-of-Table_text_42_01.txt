Solve $\begin{bmatrix} 3 & -4 & 0 \\ -4 & 15 & 4 \\ 0 & 4 & 3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} c_1 \\ c_2 \\ c_3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 6 \\ 12 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}$ using row operations, which yields $\begin{bmatrix} c_1 \\ c_2 \\ c_3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix}$.

Therefore, $\mathbf{w} = 2\mathbf{u}_1 + 1\mathbf{u}_2 - 1\mathbf{u}_3 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \\ 0 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix}$ and $\mathbf{z} = \mathbf{y} - \mathbf{w} = \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 3 \\ 3 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \\ 0 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ -1 \\ 3 \\ -6 \end{bmatrix}$.
