2. Add row 1 to row 2 (\(R_2 \leftarrow R_2 + R_1\)): \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 & | & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 3 & -2 & | & 9 \end{bmatrix}\).
3. Subtract 3 times row 1 from row 3 (\(R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 3R_1\)): \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 & | & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & 4 & | & 12 \end{bmatrix}\).
4. Subtract 4 times row 2 from row 3 (\(R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 4R_2\)): \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 & | & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & | & 0 \end{bmatrix}\).
5. Add 2 times row 2 to row 1 (\(R_1 \leftarrow R_1 + 2R_2\)): \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & | & 5 \\ 0 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & | & 0 \end{bmatrix}\).
