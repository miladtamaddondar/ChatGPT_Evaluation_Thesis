1. Write the system of equations corresponding to \( T(x_1, x_2) = (-1, 4, 9) \): \( x_1 - 2x_2 = -1 \), \( -x_1 + 3x_2 = 4 \), \( 3x_1 - 2x_2 = 9 \).  
2. Write the augmented matrix: \( \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 & | & -1 \\ -1 & 3 & | & 4 \\ 3 & -2 & | & 9 \end{bmatrix} \).  
3. Add row 1 to row 2 (\( R_2 = R_2 + R_1 \)): \( \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 & | & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 3 & -2 & | & 9 \end{bmatrix} \).  
4. Subtract 3 times row 1 from row 3 (\( R_3 = R_3 - 3R_1 \)): \( \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 & | & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & 4 & | & 12 \end{bmatrix} \).  
5. Subtract 4 times row 2 from row 3 (\( R_3 = R_3 - 4R_2 \)): \( \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 & | & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & | & 0 \end{bmatrix} \).  
6. Back-substitute from row 2: \( x_2 = 3 \); substitute into row 1: \( x_1 - 2(3) = -1 \implies x_1 = 5 \).  
7. Final answer: \( x = \begin{pmatrix} 5 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix} \).
