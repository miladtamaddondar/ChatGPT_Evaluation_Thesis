1. Let $W = \left\{ \begin{bmatrix} 3a + b \\ 4 \\ a - 5b \end{bmatrix} : a, b \in \mathbb{R} \right\}$; we first check if $W$ is closed under addition by taking two arbitrary elements $\mathbf{u} = \begin{bmatrix} 3a_1 + b_1 \\ 4 \\ a_1 - 5b_1 \end{bmatrix}$ and $\mathbf{v} = \begin{bmatrix} 3a_2 + b_2 \\ 4 \\ a_2 - 5b_2 \end{bmatrix}$ in $W$.  
2. Compute $\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{v} = \begin{bmatrix} (3a_1 + b_1) + (3a_2 + b_2) \\ 4 + 4 \\ (a_1 - 5b_1) + (a_2 - 5b_2) \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 3(a_1 + a_2) + (b_1 + b_2) \\ 8 \\ (a_1 + a_2) - 5(b_1 + b_2) \end{bmatrix}$, but the second entry is $8$, not $4$, so $W$ is not closed under addition.  
3. Since $W$ is not closed under addition, $W$ is not a vector space by the definition of a vector space.  
4. Final answer: $W$ is not a vector space; for example, $\begin{bmatrix} 3 \cdot 0 + 0 \\ 4 \\ 0 - 5 \cdot 0 \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} 3 \cdot 1 + 0 \\ 4 \\ 1 - 5 \cdot 0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 4 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 4 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 6 \\ 8 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}$, which is not in $W$ since the second entry is $8 \neq 4$.
