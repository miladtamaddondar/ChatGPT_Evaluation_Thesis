1. To find all $\mathbf{x}$ mapped to zero by $A$, we solve $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$, i.e., find the null space of $A$.
2. Write the augmented matrix: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.
3. Subtract $2$ times row 1 from row 3 to eliminate the $x_1$ term in row 3: row 3 becomes $[0, 2, -8, 6, 0]$.
4. Verify: $2 - 2\cdot1 = 0$, $-6 - 2\cdot(-4) = 2$, $6 - 2\cdot7 = -8$, $-4 - 2\cdot(-5) = 6$, $0 - 2\cdot0 = 0$.
5. Subtract $2$ times row 2 from row 3 to eliminate the $x_2$ term in row 3: row 3 becomes $[0, 0, 0, 0, 0]$.
6. Verify: $2 - 2\cdot1 = 0$, $-8 - 2\cdot(-4) = 0$, $6 - 2\cdot3 = 0$, $0 - 2\cdot0 = 0$.
7. The reduced system is now: $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.
8. Write out the equations: $x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$ and $x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0$.
9. Express $x_2$ in terms of $x_3$ and $x_4$ from the second equation: $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$.
10. Substitute $x_2$ into the first equation: $x_1 - 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.
11. Simplify: $x_1 - 16x_3 + 12x_4 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.
12. Combine like terms: $x_1 - 9x_3 + 7x_4 = 0$.
13. Solve for $x_1$: $x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4$.
14. Let $x_3 = s$ and $x_4 = t$ for $s, t \in \mathbb{R}$; then $x_2 = 4s - 3t$ and $x_1 = 9s - 7t$.
15. The general solution is $\mathbf{x} = s\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0\end{bmatrix} + t\begin{bmatrix}-7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}$ for $s, t \in \mathbb{R}$.
