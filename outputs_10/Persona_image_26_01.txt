Verify that \( A\mathbf{x} = T(\mathbf{x}) \) for all \( \mathbf{x} \): \( A\begin{pmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ x_1 + x_2 \\ x_2 + x_3 \\ x_3 + x_4 \end{pmatrix} \). 6.
