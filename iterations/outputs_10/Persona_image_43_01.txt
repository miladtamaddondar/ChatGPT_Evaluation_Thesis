1. Write the matrices: \(\mathbf{y} = \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 8 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\), \(\mathbf{u}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 2/3 \\ 1/3 \\ 2/3 \end{bmatrix}\), \(\mathbf{u}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} -2/3 \\ 2/3 \\ 1/3 \end{bmatrix}\), \(U = [\mathbf{u}_1 \ \mathbf{u}_2]\).
2. Compute \(U^T U\): \(U^T U = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{u}_1^T \mathbf{u}_1 & \mathbf{u}_1^T \mathbf{u}_2 \\ \mathbf{u}_2^T \mathbf{u}_1 & \mathbf{u}_2^T \mathbf{u}_2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\) since \(\mathbf{u}_1, \mathbf{u}_2\) are orthonormal.
3. Compute \(U U^T\): \(U U^T = \mathbf{u}_1 \mathbf{u}_1^T + \mathbf{u}_2 \mathbf{u}_2^T\).
4. Calculate \(\mathbf{u}_1 \mathbf{u}_1^T = \begin{bmatrix} 2/3 \\ 1/3 \\ 2/3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 2/3 & 1/3 & 2/3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 4/9 & 2/9 & 4/9 \\ 2/9 & 1/9 & 2/9 \\ 4/9 & 2/9 & 4/9 \end{bmatrix}\).
5. Calculate \(\mathbf{u}_2 \mathbf{u}_2^T = \begin{bmatrix} -2/3 \\ 2/3 \\ 1/3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} -2/3 & 2/3 & 1/3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 4/9 & -4/9 & -2/9 \\ -4/9 & 4/9 & 2/9 \\ -2/9 & 2/9 & 1/9 \end{bmatrix}\).
6. Add the matrices: \(U U^T = \begin{bmatrix} 4/9+4/9 & 2/9-4/9 & 4/9-2/9 \\ 2/9-4/9 & 1/9+4/9 & 2/9+2/9 \\ 4/9-2/9 & 2/9+2/9 & 4/9+1/9 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 8/9 & -2/9 & 2/9 \\ -2/9 & 5/9 & 4/9 \\ 2/9 & 4/9 & 5/9 \end{bmatrix}\).
7. The projection \(\mathrm{proj}_W \mathbf{y} = U U^T \mathbf{y}\) since \(U\) has orthonormal columns.
8. Compute \(U U^T \mathbf{y} = \begin{bmatrix} 8/9 & -2/9 & 2/9 \\ -2/9 & 5/9 & 4/9 \\ 2/9 & 4/9 & 5/9 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 8 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} (8/9)4 + (-2/9)8 + (2/9)1 \\ (-2/9)4 + (5/9)8 + (4/9)1 \\ (2/9)4 + (4/9)8 + (5/9)1 \end{bmatrix}\).
9. Calculate each entry: first row: \(32/9 - 16/9 + 2/9 = 18/9 = 2\); second row: \(-8/9 + 40/9 + 4/9 = 36/9 = 4\); third row: \(8/9 + 32/9 + 5/9 = 45/9 = 5\).
10. The final answers are: \(U^T U = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\), \(U U^T = \begin{bmatrix} 8/9 & -2/9 & 2/9 \\ -2/9 & 5/9 & 4/9 \\ 2/9 & 4/9 & 5/9 \end{bmatrix}\), \(\mathrm{proj}_W \mathbf{y} = U U^T \mathbf{y} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix}\).
