Set up the system for $\mathbf{v} = \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \\ z \end{bmatrix}$ to be orthogonal to $\mathbf{u}_1$ and $\mathbf{u}_2$: $\mathbf{u}_1 \cdot \mathbf{v} = 0$ and $\mathbf{u}_2 \cdot \mathbf{v} = 0$, which gives the equations $x + y - 2z = 0$ and $5x - y + 2z = 0$, so the augmented matrix is $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 1 & -2 & 0 \\ 5 & -1 & 2 & 0 \end{array}\right]$.

4. Row 1 $\to$ Row 1 $- $ Row 2: $\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -2 & 0 \end{array}\right]$.
5. The solution is $x = 0$, $y = 2z$, so a nonzero vector is $\mathbf{v} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 2 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}$.
