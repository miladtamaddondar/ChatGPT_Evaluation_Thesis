1. To find the characteristic polynomial, subtract $\lambda$ from the diagonal: $\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}2-\lambda & 1 \\ -1 & 4-\lambda\end{bmatrix}\right) = 0$.
2. Compute the determinant: $(2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) - (1)(-1) = (2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) + 1$.
3. Expand: $8 - 2\lambda - 4\lambda + \lambda^2 + 1 = \lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9$.
4. The characteristic polynomial is $\lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9$.
5. Set the polynomial to zero and factor: $\lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9 = 0 \implies (\lambda - 3)^2 = 0$.
6. The eigenvalues are $\lambda = 3$ (with algebraic multiplicity 2).
