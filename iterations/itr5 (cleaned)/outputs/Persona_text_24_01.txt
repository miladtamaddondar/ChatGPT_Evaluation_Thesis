2. Write the system as equations: $x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$, $x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0$, $2x_1 - 6x_2 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0$.
3. Notice that the third equation is $2$ times the first equation, so it is redundant.
4. Solve the second equation for $x_2$: $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$.
5. Substitute $x_2$ into the first equation: $x_1 - 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.
6. Expand: $x_1 - 16x_3 + 12x_4 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.
7. Combine like terms: $x_1 + (-16x_3 + 7x_3) + (12x_4 - 5x_4) = 0$.
8. Simplify: $x_1 - 9x_3 + 7x_4 = 0$.
9. Solve for $x_1$: $x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4$.
10. Express the solution in terms of free variables $x_3$ and $x_4$: $\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}9x_3 - 7x_4 \\ 4x_3 - 3x_4 \\ x_3 \\ x_4\end{bmatrix}$.
11. Write the general solution as a linear combination: $\mathbf{x} = x_3\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0\end{bmatrix} + x_4\begin{bmatrix}-7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}$, where $x_3, x_4 \in \mathbb{R}$.
12. Final answer: All $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^4$ mapped to zero by $A$ are $\mathbf{x} = x_3\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0\end{bmatrix} + x_4\begin{bmatrix}-7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}$ for $x_3, x_4 \in \mathbb{R}$.
