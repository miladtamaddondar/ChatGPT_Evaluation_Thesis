1. We are asked to find all vectors \(\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^4\) such that \(A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}\), where \(A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 \end{bmatrix}\); this is equivalent to finding the null space of \(A\). 
2. Write the system \(A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}\) as equations: \(x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0\), \(x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0\), \(2x_1 - 6x_2 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0\). 
3. Substitute \(x_2\) from the second equation into the first and third: \(x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4\). 
4. Substitute into the first equation: \(x_1 - 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0 \implies x_1 - 16x_3 + 12x_4 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0 \implies x_1 - 9x_3 + 7x_4 = 0 \implies x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4\). 
5. Substitute \(x_1\) and \(x_2\) into the third equation: \(2(9x_3 - 7x_4) - 6(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 18x_3 - 14x_4 - 24x_3 + 18x_4 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = (18 - 24 + 6)x_3 + (-14 + 18 - 4)x_4 = 0x_3 + 0x_4 = 0\), which verifies that the previous steps are correct.
 6. Therefore, the general solution is \(\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 9x_3 - 7x_4 \\ 4x_3 - 3x_4 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{bmatrix} = x_3 \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + x_4 \begin{bmatrix} -7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\), where \(x_3, x_4 \in \mathbb{R}\).
