1. Compute the dot product \(\mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v}\): \([-1, 2] \cdot [4, 6] = (-1)(4) + (2)(6) = -4 + 12 = 8\).  
2. Compute the dot product \(\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{v}\): \([4, 6] \cdot [4, 6] = (4)(4) + (6)(6) = 16 + 36 = 52\).  
3. Compute \(\frac{\mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v}}{\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{v}}\): \(\frac{8}{52} = \frac{2}{13}\).  
4. Multiply the scalar by \(\mathbf{v}\): \(\frac{2}{13}[4, 6] = [\frac{8}{13}, \frac{12}{13}]\).  
5. The answer to part (a) is \(\left(\frac{\mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v}}{\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{v}}\right)\mathbf{v} = \left[\frac{8}{13}, \frac{12}{13}\right]\).  
6. Compute the dot product \(\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{w}\): \([6, -2, 3] \cdot [3, -1, -5] = (6)(3) + (-2)(-1) + (3)(-5) = 18 + 2 - 15 = 5\).  
7. Compute the dot product \(\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{x}\): \([6, -2, 3] \cdot [6, -2, 3] = (6)(6) + (-2)(-2) + (3)(3) = 36 + 4 + 9 = 49\).  
8. Compute \(\frac{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{w}}{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{x}}\): \(\frac{5}{49}\).  
9. Multiply the scalar by \(\mathbf{x}\): \(\frac{5}{49}[6, -2, 3] = [\frac{30}{49}, -\frac{10}{49}, \frac{15}{49}]\).  
10. The answer to part (b) is \(\left(\frac{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{w}}{\mathbf{x} \cdot \mathbf{x}}\right)\mathbf{x} = \left[\frac{30}{49}, -\frac{10}{49}, \frac{15}{49}\right]\).
