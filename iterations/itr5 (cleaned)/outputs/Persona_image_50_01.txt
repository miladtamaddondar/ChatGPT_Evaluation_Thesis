1. The inner product is defined as $\langle f, g \rangle = f(-3)g(-3) + f(-1)g(-1) + f(1)g(1) + f(3)g(3)$.
2. Compute $\langle p_2, p_0 \rangle = p_2(-3)p_0(-3) + p_2(-1)p_0(-1) + p_2(1)p_0(1) + p_2(3)p_0(3) = 9 + 1 + 1 + 9 = 20$.
3. Compute $\langle p_0, p_0 \rangle = p_0(-3)^2 + p_0(-1)^2 + p_0(1)^2 + p_0(3)^2 = 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 = 4$.
4. Compute $\langle p_2, p_1 \rangle = p_2(-3)p_1(-3) + p_2(-1)p_1(-1) + p_2(1)p_1(1) + p_2(3)p_1(3) = 9(-3) + 1(-1) + 1(1) + 9(3) = -27 - 1 + 1 + 27 = 0$.
5. Compute $\langle p_1, p_1 \rangle = p_1(-3)^2 + p_1(-1)^2 + p_1(1)^2 + p_1(3)^2 = 9 + 1 + 1 + 9 = 20$.
6. The orthogonal projection of $p_2$ onto $\text{Span}\{p_0, p_1\}$ is $\frac{\langle p_2, p_0 \rangle}{\langle p_0, p_0 \rangle}p_0 + \frac{\langle p_2, p_1 \rangle}{\langle p_1, p_1 \rangle}p_1 = \frac{20}{4}p_0 + \frac{0}{20}p_1 = 5p_0$.
7. The answer to (a) is: The orthogonal projection of $p_2$ onto $\text{Span}\{p_0, p_1\}$ is $5$.

11. From step 10, $b = 0$.
12. From step 9, $20a + 4c = 0 \implies c = -5a$.
13. So $q(t) = a t^2 - 5a$.
14. Choose $a = 1$, so $q(t) = t^2 - 5$.
15. Compute $q(-3) = 9 - 5 = 4$, $q(-1) = 1 - 5 = -4$, $q(1) = 1 - 5 = -4$, $q(3) = 9 - 5 = 4$.
