1. The problem asks for an upper triangular matrix \( R \) such that \( A = QR \), where \( Q \) is given as an orthonormal matrix (from Gram-Schmidt), and \( A \) is given; by the properties of QR decomposition, \( R = Q^T A \) since \( Q \) is orthonormal (\( Q^T Q = I \)).  
2. Compute \( Q^T \): \( Q^T = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{5}{6} & \frac{1}{6} & -\frac{3}{6} & \frac{1}{6} \\ -\frac{1}{6} & \frac{5}{6} & \frac{1}{6} & \frac{3}{6} \end{bmatrix} \).  
3. Calculate \( R = Q^T A \): \( R = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{5}{6} & \frac{1}{6} & -\frac{3}{6} & \frac{1}{6} \\ -\frac{1}{6} & \frac{5}{6} & \frac{1}{6} & \frac{3}{6} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 5 & 9 \\ 1 & 7 \\ -3 & -5 \\ 1 & 5 \end{bmatrix} \).  
4. Compute the first row of \( R \): \( \left(\frac{5}{6}, \frac{1}{6}, -\frac{3}{6}, \frac{1}{6}\right) \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 1 \\ -3 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \frac{5}{6} \times 5 + \frac{1}{6} \times 1 + (-\frac{3}{6}) \times (-3) + \frac{1}{6} \times 1 = \frac{25}{6} + \frac{1}{6} + \frac{9}{6} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{36}{6} = 6 \), and \( \left(\frac{5}{6}, \frac{1}{6}, -\frac{3}{6}, \frac{1}{6}\right) \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 7 \\ -5 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} = \frac{5}{6} \times 9 + \frac{1}{6} \times 7 + (-\frac{3}{6}) \times (-5) + \frac{1}{6} \times 5 = \frac{45}{6} + \frac{7}{6} + \frac{15}{6} + \frac{5}{6} = \frac{72}{6} = 12 \).  
5. Compute the second row of \( R \): \( \left(-\frac{1}{6}, \frac{5}{6}, \frac{1}{6}, \frac{3}{6}\right) \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 1 \\ -3 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = -\frac{1}{6} \times 5 + \frac{5}{6} \times 1 + \frac{1}{6} \times (-3) + \frac{3}{6} \times 1 = -\frac{5}{6} + \frac{5}{6} - \frac{3}{6} + \frac{3}{6} = 0 \), and \( \left(-\frac{1}{6}, \frac{5}{6}, \frac{1}{6}, \frac{3}{6}\right) \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 7 \\ -5 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} = -\frac{1}{6} \times 9 + \frac{5}{6} \times 7 + \frac{1}{6} \times (-5) + \frac{3}{6} \times 5 = -\frac{9}{6} + \frac{35}{6} - \frac{5}{6} + \frac{15}{6} = \frac{36}{6} = 6 \).  
6. Assemble \( R \): \( R = \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 12 \\ 0 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \), which is upper triangular as required by the QR decomposition.  
7. Verify by checking \( QR \): \( Q \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 12 \\ 0 & 6 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 5 & 9 \\ 1 & 7 \\ -3 & -5 \\ 1 & 5 \end{bmatrix} = A \), confirming the correctness of the solution.
