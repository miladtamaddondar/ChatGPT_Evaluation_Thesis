1. Write the basis polynomials and the target polynomial: The basis is \( \mathcal{B} = \{1,\, 2t,\, -2 + 4t^2,\, -12t + 8t^3\} \), and \( p(t) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3 \).
2. Set up the equation \( a_1(1) + a_2(2t) + a_3(-2 + 4t^2) + a_4(-12t + 8t^3) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3 \).
3. Expand and collect like terms: \( a_1 + 2a_2 t + (-2a_3) + 4a_3 t^2 + (-12a_4 t) + 8a_4 t^3 = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3 \).
4. Group coefficients: Constant: \( a_1 - 2a_3 = 7 \); \( t \): \( 2a_2 - 12a_4 = -12 \); \( t^2 \): \( 4a_3 = -8 \); \( t^3 \): \( 8a_4 = 12 \).
5. Write the augmented matrix: \( \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & -12 \\ 0 & 0 & 4 & 0 & -8 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 8 & 12 \end{array}\right] \).
6. Row 3: Divide by 4 to get leading 1: \( R_3 \to \frac{1}{4}R_3 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & -12 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 8 & 12 \end{array}\right] \).
7. Row 4: Divide by 8 to get leading 1: \( R_4 \to \frac{1}{8}R_4 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & -12 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right] \).
8. Row 2: Eliminate \( a_4 \) using row 4: \( R_2 \to R_2 + 12R_4 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & 0 & 6 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right] \).
9. Row 2: Divide by 2 to get leading 1: \( R_2 \to \frac{1}{2}R_2 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right] \).
10. Row 1: Eliminate \( a_3 \) using row 3: \( R_1 \to R_1 + 2R_3 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right] \).
11. The solution is \( [a_1, a_2, a_3, a_4] = [3, 3, -2, \frac{3}{2}] \), so the coordinate vector of \( p \) relative to \( \mathcal{B} \) is \( \boxed{\begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{bmatrix}} \).
