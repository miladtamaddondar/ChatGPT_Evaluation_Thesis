1. To find the characteristic polynomial, compute $\det(A - \lambda I)$ for $A = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 1 \\ -1 & 4\end{bmatrix}$: $A - \lambda I = \begin{bmatrix}2-\lambda & 1 \\ -1 & 4-\lambda\end{bmatrix}$.
2. Compute the determinant: $(2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) - (1)(-1) = (2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) + 1$.
3. Expand: $(2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) + 1 = 8 - 2\lambda - 4\lambda + \lambda^2 + 1 = \lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9$.
4. The characteristic polynomial is $\boxed{\lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9}$.
5. Set the characteristic polynomial to zero to find eigenvalues: $\lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9 = 0$.
6. Factor: $(\lambda - 3)^2 = 0$.
7. The eigenvalues are $\boxed{\lambda = 3}$ (with multiplicity 2).
