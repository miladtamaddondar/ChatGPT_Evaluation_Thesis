5. Multiply both sides by the inverse to solve for \( \mathbf{x} \): \( \begin{pmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 5 & -1 \\ -4 & 1 \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} 3 \\ 8 \end{pmatrix} \).  
6. Compute \( x_1 = 5 \cdot 3 + (-1) \cdot 8 = 15 - 8 = 7 \) and \( x_2 = -4 \cdot 3 + 1 \cdot 8 = -12 + 8 = -4 \).
