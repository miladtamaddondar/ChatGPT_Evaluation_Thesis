1. Write \( U \) as a \( 3 \times 2 \) matrix: \( U = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \).
2. Compute \( U^T U \): \( U^T U = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ -\frac{2}{3} & \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \).
3. Compute \( U U^T \): \( U U^T = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ -\frac{2}{3} & \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} & 0 & \frac{2}{9} \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ \frac{2}{9} & 0 & \frac{8}{9} \end{bmatrix} \).
4. Compute \( (U U^T) y \): \( (U U^T) y = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} & 0 & \frac{2}{9} \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ \frac{2}{9} & 0 & \frac{8}{9} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 8 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} \cdot 4 + 0 \cdot 8 + \frac{2}{9} \cdot 1 \\ 0 \cdot 4 + 1 \cdot 8 + 0 \cdot 1 \\ \frac{2}{9} \cdot 4 + 0 \cdot 8 + \frac{8}{9} \cdot 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{32}{9} + \frac{2}{9} \\ 8 \\ \frac{8}{9} + \frac{8}{9} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{34}{9} \\ 8 \\ \frac{16}{9} \end{bmatrix} \).
5. The projection \(\mathrm{proj}_W y = (U U^T) y = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{34}{9} \\ 8 \\ \frac{16}{9} \end{bmatrix} \).
