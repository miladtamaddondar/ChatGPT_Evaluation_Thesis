1. Write the equation to solve: Find all \(\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^4\) such that \(A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}\), where \(A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 \end{bmatrix}\). 
2. Let \(\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{bmatrix}\), so the system is: \(\begin{cases} x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0 \\ x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0 \\ 2x_1 - 6x_2 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0 \end{cases}\). 
3. Verify the third equation: Multiply the first equation by 2 to get \(2x_1 - 8x_2 + 14x_3 - 10x_4 = 0\), subtract the third equation to get \((-8x_2 + 14x_3 - 10x_4) - (-6x_2 + 6x_3 - 4x_4) = -2x_2 + 8x_3 - 6x_4 = 0\), so the third equation is not a linear combination of the first two and is needed. 
4. Solve the second equation for \(x_2\): \(x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4\). 
5. Substitute \(x_2\) into the first equation: \(x_1 - 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0\), which simplifies to \(x_1 - 16x_3 + 12x_4 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0\), so \(x_1 - 9x_3 + 7x_4 = 0\), thus \(x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4\). 
6. Substitute \(x_1\) and \(x_2\) into the third equation: \(2(9x_3 - 7x_4) - 6(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0\), which gives \(18x_3 - 14x_4 - 24x_3 + 18x_4 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0\), so \((18 - 24 + 6)x_3 + (-14 + 18 - 4)x_4 = 0\), which simplifies to \(0x_3 + 0x_4 = 0\), confirming the third equation is satisfied for all \(x_3, x_4\). 
7. The general solution is \(\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix} 9x_3 - 7x_4 \\ 4x_3 - 3x_4 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{bmatrix}\), where \(x_3, x_4 \in \mathbb{R}\). 
8. In vector form, the null space is \(\mathbf{x} = x_3 \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + x_4 \begin{bmatrix} -7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\), for \(x_3, x_4 \in \mathbb{R}\).
