3. Row 4 $\to$ Row 4 + Row 3: $\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c}0 & -2 & 6 & -1 & 0 \\0 & -2 & h & 0 & 0 \\0 & 0 & 0 & 4 & 0 \\0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0\end{array}\right]$.

6. The system now has a free variable $x_1$ and possibly $x_3$ if the second row becomes dependent; for the eigenspace to be two-dimensional, the second row must be a multiple of the first, so set $h=6$.
