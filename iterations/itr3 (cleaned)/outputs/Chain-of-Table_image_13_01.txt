1. Write the equation \((A - 4I)\vec{x} = 0\): \[A - 4I = \begin{bmatrix} 3-4 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 1 & 3-4 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 1-4 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 4-4 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}\]
2. Write the augmented matrix for the homogeneous system: \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\)
3. Add row 1 to row 2: \(R_2 \to R_2 + R_1\): \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\)
4. Add row 2 to row 3: \(R_3 \to R_3 + R_2\): \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\)
5. Multiply row 1 and row 2 by \(-1\): \(R_1 \to -R_1, R_2 \to -R_2\): \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\)
6. Write the system: \(x_1 - 2x_3 = 0\), \(x_2 - 3x_3 = 0\), so \(x_1 = 2x_3\), \(x_2 = 3x_3\), \(x_4\) is free.
7. General solution: \(\vec{x} = \begin{bmatrix} 2x_3 \\ 3x_3 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{bmatrix} = x_3\begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + x_4\begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\)
8. A basis for the eigenspace is \(\left\{\begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 3 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\right\}\)
