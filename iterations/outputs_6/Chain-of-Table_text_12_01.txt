1. The characteristic polynomial is found by computing $\det(A - \lambda I)$ for $A = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 1 \\ -1 & 4\end{bmatrix}$: $\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}2-\lambda & 1 \\ -1 & 4-\lambda\end{bmatrix}\right)$.
2. Compute the determinant: $(2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) - (1)(-1) = (2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) + 1$.
3. Expand: $(2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) + 1 = 8 - 2\lambda - 4\lambda + \lambda^2 + 1 = \lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9$.
4. The characteristic polynomial is $\lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9$.
5. Factor the polynomial: $\lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9 = (\lambda - 3)^2$.
6. The eigenvalues are $\lambda = 3$ (with algebraic multiplicity 2).
