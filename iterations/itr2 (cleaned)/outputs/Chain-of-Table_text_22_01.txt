1. The standard matrix for a rotation by angle $\theta$ counterclockwise is $\begin{bmatrix} \cos\theta & -\sin\theta \\ \sin\theta & \cos\theta \end{bmatrix}$.
2. Substitute $\theta = \frac{3\pi}{2}$: $\cos\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) = 0$, $\sin\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) = -1$.
3. Therefore, the standard matrix is $\begin{bmatrix} 0 & -(-1) \\ -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.
