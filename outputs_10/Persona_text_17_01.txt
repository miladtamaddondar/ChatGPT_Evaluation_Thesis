a. To find the image under $T$ of $p(t) = 5 + 3t$, we evaluate $p$ at $-1$, $0$, and $1$: $p(-1) = 5 + 3(-1) = 2$, $p(0) = 5 + 3(0) = 5$, $p(1) = 5 + 3(1) = 8$.

To find the matrix for $T$ relative to the basis $\{1, t, t^2\}$ for $\mathbb{P}_2$ and the standard basis for $\mathbb{R}^3$, we first compute $T(1) = \begin{bmatrix}1\\1\\1\end{bmatrix}$, $T(t) = \begin{bmatrix}-1\\0\\1\end{bmatrix}$, and $T(t^2) = \begin{bmatrix}1\\0\\1\end{bmatrix}$, so the matrix is $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -1 & 1\\1 & 0 & 0\\1 & 1 & 1\end{bmatrix}$.
