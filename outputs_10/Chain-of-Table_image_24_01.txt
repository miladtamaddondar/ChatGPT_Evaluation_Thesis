1. Write the augmented matrix for the homogeneous system \(A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}\): \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).  
2. Eliminate the \(2\) in row 3, column 1 by replacing row 3 with row 3 minus 2 times row 1: \(R_3 \to R_3 - 2R_1\), so \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -8 & 6 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).  
3. Eliminate the \(2\) in row 3, column 2 by replacing row 3 with row 3 minus 2 times row 2: \(R_3 \to R_3 - 2R_2\), so \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).  
4. Write the system of equations from the row-reduced matrix: \(x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0\), \(x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0\).  
5. Express leading variables in terms of free variables \(x_3\) and \(x_4\): \(x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4\), \(x_1 = 4x_2 - 7x_3 + 5x_4\).  
6. Substitute \(x_2\) into \(x_1\): \(x_1 = 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) - 7x_3 + 5x_4 = 16x_3 - 12x_4 - 7x_3 + 5x_4 = 9x_3 - 7x_4\).  
7. Write the general solution: \(\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{bmatrix} = x_3 \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + x_4 \begin{bmatrix} -7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\), where \(x_3, x_4 \in \mathbb{R}\).
