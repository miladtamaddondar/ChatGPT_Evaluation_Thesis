Set up the equation $a_1(1) + a_2(2t) + a_3(-2 + 4t^2) + a_4(-12t + 8t^3) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3$ and collect like terms to get the system: $a_1 - 2a_3 = 7$, $2a_2 - 12a_4 = -12$, $4a_3 = -8$, $8a_4 = 12$, which gives the augmented matrix $\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & -12 \\ 0 & 0 & 4 & 0 & -8 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 8 & 12 \end{array}\right]$.
2. Divide row 3 by 4 and row 4 by 8 to get leading 1s: $\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & -12 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right]$.
3. Use row 4 to eliminate $a_4$ from row 2: add $12$ times row 4 to row 2: $\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & 0 & 6 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right]$.
4. Divide row 2 by 2 to get a leading 1: $\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & 7 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right]$.
5. Use row 3 to eliminate $a_3$ from row 1: add $2$ times row 3 to row 1: $\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right]$.
6. The solution is $\left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right}$, so the coordinate vector of $p$ relative to $\mathcal{B}$ is $\left[\begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{array}\right}$.
