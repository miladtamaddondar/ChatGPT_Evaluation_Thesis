1. Write the equation $T(x_1, x_2) = (-1, 4, 9)$ as a system: $x_1 - 2x_2 = -1$, $-x_1 + 3x_2 = 4$, $3x_1 - 2x_2 = 9$.
2. Use the first equation to express $x_1$ in terms of $x_2$: $x_1 = -1 + 2x_2$.
3. Substitute $x_1$ into the second equation: $-(-1 + 2x_2) + 3x_2 = 4$.
4. Simplify the second equation: $1 - 2x_2 + 3x_2 = 4$.
5. Combine like terms: $1 + x_2 = 4$.
6. Solve for $x_2$: $x_2 = 3$.
7. Substitute $x_2 = 3$ into $x_1 = -1 + 2x_2$: $x_1 = -1 + 2 \times 3 = 5$.
8. Check the third equation with $x_1 = 5$, $x_2 = 3$: $3 \times 5 - 2 \times 3 = 15 - 6 = 9$.
9. The solution is $\mathbf{x} = (5, 3)$.
