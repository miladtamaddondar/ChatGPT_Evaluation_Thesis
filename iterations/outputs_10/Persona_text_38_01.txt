1. Write the basis $\mathcal{B} = \{1,\, 2t,\, -2 + 4t^2,\, -12t + 8t^3\}$ and express $p(t)$ as $a_1(1) + a_2(2t) + a_3(-2 + 4t^2) + a_4(-12t + 8t^3)$.
2. Expand the right side: $a_1 + 2a_2 t + a_3(-2) + a_3(4t^2) + a_4(-12t) + a_4(8t^3) = a_1 - 2a_3 + (2a_2 - 12a_4)t + 4a_3 t^2 + 8a_4 t^3$.
3. Match coefficients with $p(t) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3$: $a_1 - 2a_3 = 7$, $2a_2 - 12a_4 = -12$, $4a_3 = -8$, $8a_4 = 12$.
4. Solve $4a_3 = -8$ to get $a_3 = -2$.
5. Solve $8a_4 = 12$ to get $a_4 = \frac{3}{2}$.
6. Substitute $a_3$ into $a_1 - 2a_3 = 7$: $a_1 - 2(-2) = 7 \implies a_1 + 4 = 7 \implies a_1 = 3$.
7. Substitute $a_4$ into $2a_2 - 12a_4 = -12$: $2a_2 - 12(\frac{3}{2}) = -12 \implies 2a_2 - 18 = -12 \implies 2a_2 = 6 \implies a_2 = 3$.
8. The coordinate vector of $p$ relative to $\mathcal{B}$ is $\begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{bmatrix}$.
