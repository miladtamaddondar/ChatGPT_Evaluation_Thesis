1. Write the matrix \( A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & 5 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \) and set up the characteristic equation \( \det(A - \lambda I) = 0 \).
2. Compute \( A - \lambda I = \begin{bmatrix} 0-\lambda & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 2-\lambda & 5 \\ 0 & 0 & 1-\lambda \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -\lambda & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 2-\lambda & 5 \\ 0 & 0 & 1-\lambda \end{bmatrix} \).
3. The determinant is \( \det(A - \lambda I) = (-\lambda) \cdot \det \begin{bmatrix} 2-\lambda & 5 \\ 0 & 1-\lambda \end{bmatrix} \).
4. Compute the 2x2 determinant: \( \det \begin{bmatrix} 2-\lambda & 5 \\ 0 & 1-\lambda \end{bmatrix} = (2-\lambda)(1-\lambda) - 0 = (2-\lambda)(1-\lambda) \).
5. So, \( \det(A - \lambda I) = (-\lambda)(2-\lambda)(1-\lambda) \).
6. Set the characteristic equation to zero: \( (-\lambda)(2-\lambda)(1-\lambda) = 0 \).
7. The eigenvalues are \( \boxed{0, 1, 2} \).
