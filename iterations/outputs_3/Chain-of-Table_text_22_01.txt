1. The standard matrix for a rotation by angle $\theta$ counterclockwise is $\begin{bmatrix}\cos\theta & -\sin\theta \\ \sin\theta & \cos\theta\end{bmatrix}$.
2. Substitute $\theta = \frac{3\pi}{2}$: $\cos\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) = 0$, $\sin\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) = -1$, so the matrix is $\begin{bmatrix}0 & -(-1) \\ -1 & 0\end{bmatrix}$.
3. Simplify $-(-1)$ to $1$, so the standard matrix is $\boxed{\begin{bmatrix}0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0\end{bmatrix}}$.
