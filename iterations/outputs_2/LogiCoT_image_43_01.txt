1. Write the matrix \( U \) with columns \( \mathbf{u}_1 \) and \( \mathbf{u}_2 \): \( U = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \).  
2. Compute \( U^T U \): \( U^T U = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ -\frac{2}{3} & \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \) (since \( \mathbf{u}_1 \) and \( \mathbf{u}_2 \) are orthonormal, verify: dot products are 1 for same vector, 0 for different).  
3. Compute \( U U^T \): \( U U^T = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ -\frac{2}{3} & \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} (\frac{2}{3})^2 + (-\frac{2}{3})^2 & \frac{2}{3}\frac{1}{3} + (-\frac{2}{3})\frac{2}{3} & \frac{2}{3}\frac{2}{3} + (-\frac{2}{3})\frac{1}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3}\frac{2}{3} + \frac{2}{3}(-\frac{2}{3}) & (\frac{1}{3})^2 + (\frac{2}{3})^2 & \frac{1}{3}\frac{2}{3} + \frac{2}{3}\frac{1}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3}\frac{2}{3} + \frac{1}{3}(-\frac{2}{3}) & \frac{2}{3}\frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{3}\frac{2}{3} & (\frac{2}{3})^2 + (\frac{1}{3})^2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} & -\frac{2}{9} & \frac{4}{9} \\ -\frac{2}{9} & \frac{5}{9} & \frac{8}{9} \\ \frac{4}{9} & \frac{8}{9} & \frac{5}{9} \end{bmatrix} \) (verify by direct multiplication).  
4. Compute \( \text{proj}_W \mathbf{y} = U U^T \mathbf{y} \): \( U U^T \mathbf{y} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} & -\frac{2}{9} & \frac{4}{9} \\ -\frac{2}{9} & \frac{5}{9} & \frac{8}{9} \\ \frac{4}{9} & \frac{8}{9} & \frac{5}{9} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 8 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} \cdot 4 + (-\frac{2}{9}) \cdot 8 + \frac{4}{9} \cdot 1 \\ -\frac{2}{9} \cdot 4 + \frac{5}{9} \cdot 8 + \frac{8}{9} \cdot 1 \\ \frac{4}{9} \cdot 4 + \frac{8}{9} \cdot 8 + \frac{5}{9} \cdot 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{32}{9} - \frac{16}{9} + \frac{4}{9} \\ -\frac{8}{9} + \frac{40}{9} + \frac{8}{9} \\ \frac{16}{9} + \frac{64}{9} + \frac{5}{9} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{20}{9} \\ \frac{40}{9} \\ \frac{85}{9} \end{bmatrix} \) (verify by arithmetic: first entry: \( \frac{32-16+4}{9} = \frac{20}{9} \), second: \( \frac{-8+40+8}{9} = \frac{40}{9} \), third: \( \frac{16+64+5}{9} = \frac{85}{9} \)).  
5. The answer to both \( \text{proj}_W \mathbf{y} \) and \( (U U^T)\mathbf{y} \) is \( \begin{bmatrix} \frac{20}{9} \\ \frac{40}{9} \\ \frac{85}{9} \end{bmatrix} \).
