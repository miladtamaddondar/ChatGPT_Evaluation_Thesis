2. Compute $R = Q^T A$: $R = \begin{bmatrix} 5/6 & 1/6 & -3/6 & 1/6 \\ -1/6 & 5/6 & 1/6 & 3/6 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 5 & 9 \\ 1 & 7 \\ -3 & -5 \\ 1 & 5 \end{bmatrix}$.
3. Calculate the first row of $R$: $[5/6, 1/6, -3/6, 1/6] \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 1 \\ -3 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = (5/6)5 + (1/6)1 + (-3/6)(-3) + (1/6)1 = 25/6 + 1/6 + 9/6 + 1/6 = 36/6 = 6$; $[5/6, 1/6, -3/6, 1/6] \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 7 \\ -5 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} = (5/6)9 + (1/6)7 + (-3/6)(-5) + (1/6)5 = 45/6 + 7/6 + 15/6 + 5/6 = 72/6 = 12$.
4. Calculate the second row of $R$: $[-1/6, 5/6, 1/6, 3/6] \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 1 \\ -3 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = (-1/6)5 + (5/6)1 + (1/6)(-3) + (3/6)1 = -5/6 + 5/6 -3/6 + 3/6 = 0$; $[-1/6, 5/6, 1/6, 3/6] \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 7 \\ -5 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} = (-1/6)9 + (5/6)7 + (1/6)(-5) + (3/6)5 = -9/6 + 35/6 -5/6 + 15/6 = (35 - 9 - 5 + 15)/6 = 36/6 = 6$.
5. Assemble $R$: $R = \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 12 \\ 0 & 6 \end{bmatrix}$.
6. Check $A = QR$: $Q \cdot R = \begin{bmatrix} 5/6 & -1/6 \\ 1/6 & 5/6 \\ -3/6 & 1/6 \\ 1/6 & 3/6 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 12 \\ 0 & 6 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} (5/6)6 + (-1/6)0 & (5/6)12 + (-1/6)6 \\ (1/6)6 + (5/6)0 & (1/6)12 + (5/6)6 \\ (-3/6)6 + (1/6)0 & (-3/6)12 + (1/6)6 \\ (1/6)6 + (3/6)0 & (1/6)12 + (3/6)6 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 5 & 9 \\ 1 & 7 \\ -3 & -5 \\ 1 & 5 \end{bmatrix} = A$.
