1. Write \( p(t) = 1 + 4t + 7t^2 \) as a linear combination of the basis: \( c_1(1 + t^2) + c_2(t + t^2) + c_3(1 + 2t + t^2) = 1 + 4t + 7t^2 \).
2. Expand: \( (c_1 + c_3) + (c_2 + 2c_3)t + (c_1 + c_2 + c_3)t^2 = 1 + 4t + 7t^2 \).
3. Set up the system: \( c_1 + c_3 = 1 \), \( c_2 + 2c_3 = 4 \), \( c_1 + c_2 + c_3 = 7 \).
4. Write the augmented matrix: \( \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 4 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & 7 \end{array}\right] \).
5. Subtract row 1 from row 3: \( R_3 \to R_3 - R_1 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 4 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 6 \end{array}\right] \).
6. Subtract row 2 from row 3: \( R_3 \to R_3 - R_2 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 4 \\ 0 & 0 & -2 & 2 \end{array}\right] \).
7. Divide row 3 by -2: \( R_3 \to \frac{1}{-2}R_3 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 4 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 \end{array}\right] \).
8. Subtract row 3 from row 1: \( R_1 \to R_1 - R_3 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 4 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 \end{array}\right] \).
9. Subtract 2 times row 3 from row 2: \( R_2 \to R_2 - 2R_3 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 6 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 \end{array}\right] \).
