1. Compute \( T(\mathbf{b}_1) = A\mathbf{b}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \).
2. Set up the equation \( T(\mathbf{b}_1) = c_1 \mathbf{b}_1 + c_2 \mathbf{b}_2 \), so \( \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} = c_1 \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} + c_2 \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} \).
3. Write the augmented matrix for the system: \( \left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 5 & 2 \\ 1 & 4 & 2 \end{array}\right] \).
4. Subtract row 1 from row 2: \( R_2 \to R_2 - R_1 \), resulting in \( \left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 5 & 2 \\ 0 & -1 & 0 \end{array}\right] \).
5. Multiply row 2 by \(-1\): \( R_2 \to -R_2 \), resulting in \( \left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 5 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \end{array}\right] \).
6. Subtract 5 times row 2 from row 1: \( R_1 \to R_1 - 5R_2 \), resulting in \( \left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \end{array}\right] \).
7. Solution: \( c_1 = 2, c_2 = 0 \), so \( [T(\mathbf{b}_1)]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \).
8. Compute \( T(\mathbf{b}_2) = A\mathbf{b}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} \).
9. Set up the equation \( T(\mathbf{b}_2) = d_1 \mathbf{b}_1 + d_2 \mathbf{b}_2 \), so \( \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} = d_1 \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} + d_2 \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} \).
10. Write the augmented matrix for the system: \( \left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 5 & 9 \\ 1 & 4 & 7 \end{array}\right] \).
11. Subtract row 1 from row 2: \( R_2 \to R_2 - R_1 \), resulting in \( \left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 5 & 9 \\ 0 & -1 & -2 \end{array}\right] \).
12. Multiply row 2 by \(-1\): \( R_2 \to -R_2 \), resulting in \( \left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 5 & 9 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 \end{array}\right] \).
13. Subtract 5 times row 2 from row 1: \( R_1 \to R_1 - 5R_2 \), resulting in \( \left[\begin{array}{cc|c} 1 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 \end{array}\right] \).
14. Solution: \( d_1 = -1, d_2 = 2 \), so \( [T(\mathbf{b}_2)]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \).
15. The \(\mathcal{B}\)-matrix for \(T\) is \( \boxed{\begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix}} \).
