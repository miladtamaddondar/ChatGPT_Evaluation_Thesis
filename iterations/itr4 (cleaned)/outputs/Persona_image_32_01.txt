4. Calculate the second entry: \(6 \cdot 1 + (-2) \cdot 3 + 0 \cdot (-4) = 6 - 6 + 0 = 0\).  
5. Calculate the third entry: \((-8) \cdot 1 + 4 \cdot 3 + 1 \cdot (-4) = -8 + 12 - 4 = 0\).  
6. Since \(A\mathbf{w} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}\), \(\mathbf{w}\) is in \(\text{Nul } A\).
