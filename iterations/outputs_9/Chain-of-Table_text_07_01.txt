Recognize that \( Q r_1, Q r_2, \dots, Q r_p \) are the images of \( r_1, \dots, r_p \) under multiplication by \( Q \), so the matrix \(\left[ Q r_1 \; Q r_2 \; \dots \; Q r_p \right]\) can be written as \( Q \) times the matrix whose columns are \( r_1, \dots, r_p \): \(\left[ Q r_1 \; Q r_2 \; \dots \; Q r_p \right] = Q \left[ r_1 \; r_2 \; \dots \; r_p \right]\).
