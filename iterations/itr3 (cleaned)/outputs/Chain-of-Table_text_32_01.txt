1. To determine if $\mathbf{w}$ is in $\text{Nul }A$, set up the equation $A\mathbf{w} = \mathbf{0}$, or equivalently, check if $A\mathbf{w} = \mathbf{0}$ when $\mathbf{w} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 3 \\ -4 \end{bmatrix}$.
2. Compute $A\mathbf{w}$: $A\mathbf{w} = \begin{bmatrix} 3 & -5 & -3 \\ 6 & -2 & 0 \\ -8 & 4 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 3 \\ -4 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 3(1) + (-5)(3) + (-3)(-4) \\ 6(1) + (-2)(3) + 0(-4) \\ -8(1) + 4(3) + 1(-4) \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 3 - 15 + 12 \\ 6 - 6 + 0 \\ -8 + 12 - 4 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}$.
3. Since $A\mathbf{w} = \mathbf{0}$, $\mathbf{w}$ is in $\text{Nul }A$.
