1. Compute \(T(\mathbf{b}_1) = A\mathbf{b}_1 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}\).
2. Express \(\begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}\) as a linear combination of \(\mathbf{b}_1\) and \(\mathbf{b}_2\): Set up the augmented matrix \(\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 2 \\ 1 & 4 & 2\end{array}\right]\).
3. Row 2 minus Row 1: \(\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 2 \\ 0 & -1 & 0\end{array}\right]\).
4. Row 2 times \(-1\): \(\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0\end{array}\right]\).
5. Row 1 minus 5 times Row 2: \(\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0\end{array}\right]\), so \(T(\mathbf{b}_1) = 2\mathbf{b}_1 + 0\mathbf{b}_2\).
6. Compute \(T(\mathbf{b}_2) = A\mathbf{b}_2 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix}\).
7. Express \(\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix}\) as a linear combination of \(\mathbf{b}_1\) and \(\mathbf{b}_2\): Set up the augmented matrix \(\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 9 \\ 1 & 4 & 7\end{array}\right]\).
8. Row 2 minus Row 1: \(\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 9 \\ 0 & -1 & -2\end{array}\right]\).
9. Row 2 times \(-1\): \(\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 5 & 9 \\ 0 & 1 & 2\end{array}\right]\).
10. Row 1 minus 5 times Row 2: \(\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}1 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2\end{array}\right]\), so \(T(\mathbf{b}_2) = -1\mathbf{b}_1 + 2\mathbf{b}_2\).
11. The \(\mathcal{B}\)-matrix for \(T\) is \(\begin{bmatrix}2 & -1 \\ 0 & 2\end{bmatrix}\).
