1. To find all $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^4$ mapped to the zero vector by $A$, we solve $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$, i.e., find the null space of $A$.
2. Let $\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4\end{bmatrix}$, then $A\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}0 \\ 0 \\ 0\end{bmatrix}$.
3. This gives the system: $1x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$, $x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0$, $2x_1 - 6x_2 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0$.
4. From the second equation, $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$.
5. Substitute $x_2$ into the first equation: $x_1 - 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0 \implies x_1 - 16x_3 + 12x_4 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0 \implies x_1 - 9x_3 + 7x_4 = 0$.
6. Thus, $x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4$.
7. Substitute $x_1$ and $x_2$ into the third equation: $2x_1 - 6x_2 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0 \implies 2(9x_3 - 7x_4) - 6(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0$.
8. Expand: $18x_3 - 14x_4 - 24x_3 + 18x_4 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = (18 - 24 + 6)x_3 + (-14 + 18 - 4)x_4 = 0x_3 + 0x_4 = 0$.
9. The third equation is always satisfied, so the general solution is $x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4$, $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$, $x_3 = x_3$, $x_4 = x_4$.
10. Therefore, all $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^4$ mapped to zero by $A$ are $\mathbf{x} = x_3\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0\end{bmatrix} + x_4\begin{bmatrix}-7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}$ for $x_3, x_4 \in \mathbb{R}$.
