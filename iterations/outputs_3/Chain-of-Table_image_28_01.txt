1. Write the transformation \( T(\mathbf{x}) = x_1 \mathbf{v}_1 + x_2 \mathbf{v}_2 \) as \( T(\mathbf{x}) = x_1 \begin{bmatrix}-2 \\ 5\end{bmatrix} + x_2 \begin{bmatrix}7 \\ -3\end{bmatrix} \). 
2. Combine into a single matrix-vector product: \( T(\mathbf{x}) = \begin{bmatrix}-2 & 7 \\ 5 & -3\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}x_1 \\ x_2\end{bmatrix} \). 
3. Therefore, the matrix \( A \) such that \( T(\mathbf{x}) = A\mathbf{x} \) is \( \boxed{\begin{bmatrix}-2 & 7 \\ 5 & -3\end{bmatrix}} \).
