1. Write the system as equations: $x_1 + x_2 = 3$, $4x_1 + 5x_2 = 8$.
2. Write the augmented matrix: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 4 & 5 & | & 8 \end{bmatrix}$.
3. $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 4R_1$: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & | & -4 \end{bmatrix}$.
4. $R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - R_2$: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & | & 7 \\ 0 & 1 & | & -4 \end{bmatrix}$.
5. Solution: $\mathbf{x} = \begin{pmatrix} 7 \\ -4 \end{pmatrix}$.
