1. Write the augmented matrix for the homogeneous system \(A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}\): \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).
2. Replace row 3 with row 3 minus 2 times row 1: \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -8 & 6 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).
3. Replace row 3 with row 3 minus 2 times row 2: \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).
4. The system is now in row-echelon form; write the equations: \(x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0\), \(x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0\).
5. Express \(x_1\) and \(x_2\) in terms of free variables \(x_3 = s\), \(x_4 = t\): \(x_2 = 4s - 3t\), \(x_1 = -7s + 5t + 4x_2 = -7s + 5t + 4(4s - 3t) = -7s + 5t + 16s - 12t = 9s - 7t\).
6. The solution set is \(\mathbf{x} = s\begin{pmatrix}9\\4\\1\\0\end{pmatrix} + t\begin{pmatrix}-7\\-3\\0\\1\end{pmatrix}\), where \(s, t \in \mathbb{R}\).
