1. Since \(T\) maps \(\mathbf{e}_1\) to \(\mathbf{y}_1\) and \(\mathbf{e}_2\) to \(\mathbf{y}_2\), the matrix of \(T\) is \(A = [\mathbf{y}_1 \ \mathbf{y}_2] = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix}\).
2. To find the image of \(\begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix}\), compute \(A \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix}\).
3. Calculate the first entry: \(2 \cdot 5 + (-1) \cdot (-3) = 10 + 3 = 13\).
4. Calculate the second entry: \(5 \cdot 5 + 6 \cdot (-3) = 25 - 18 = 7\).
5. Thus, the image of \(\begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix}\) is \(\begin{bmatrix} 13 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix}\).
6. For a general vector \(\begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix}\), compute \(A \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix}\).
7. The first entry is \(2x_1 - x_2\), and the second entry is \(5x_1 + 6x_2\).
8. Thus, the image of \(\begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix}\) is \(\begin{bmatrix} 2x_1 - x_2 \\ 5x_1 + 6x_2 \end{bmatrix}\).
