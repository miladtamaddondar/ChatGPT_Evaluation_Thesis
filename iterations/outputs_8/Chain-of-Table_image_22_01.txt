1. The standard matrix for a counterclockwise rotation by angle \(\theta\) is \(\begin{bmatrix} \cos\theta & -\sin\theta \\ \sin\theta & \cos\theta \end{bmatrix}\).
2. Substitute \(\theta = \frac{3\pi}{2}\): \(\cos\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) = 0\), \(\sin\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) = -1\).
3. The standard matrix is \(\begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}\).
