1. To find the $\mathcal{B}$-matrix for $T$, we need to find $[T]_{\mathcal{B}}$ such that $[T(\mathbf{x})]_{\mathcal{B}} = [T]_{\mathcal{B}}[\mathbf{x}]_{\mathcal{B}}$; this means we compute $[T(\mathbf{b}_1)]_{\mathcal{B}}$ and $[T(\mathbf{b}_2)]_{\mathcal{B}}$ as columns.  
2. Compute $T(\mathbf{b}_1) = A\mathbf{b}_1 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$.  
3. Set up the equation $T(\mathbf{b}_1) = c_1\mathbf{b}_1 + c_2\mathbf{b}_2$ or $\begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 2\end{bmatrix} = c_1\begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} + c_2\begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix}$, which gives the augmented matrix:  
\[
\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}
1 & 5 & 2 \\
1 & 4 & 2 \\
\end{array}\right]
\]  
4. Subtract row 1 from row 2: $R_2 \to R_2 - R_1$ gives  
\[
\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}
1 & 5 & 2 \\
0 & -1 & 0 \\
\end{array}\right]
\]  
5. Multiply row 2 by $-1$: $R_2 \to -R_2$ gives  
\[
\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}
1 & 5 & 2 \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
\end{array}\right]
\]  
6. Subtract $5 \times$ row 2 from row 1: $R_1 \to R_1 - 5R_2$ gives  
\[
\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}
1 & 0 & 2 \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
\end{array}\right]
\]  
7. Thus, $[T(\mathbf{b}_1)]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix}2 \\ 0\end{bmatrix}$.  
8. Compute $T(\mathbf{b}_2) = A\mathbf{b}_2 = \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix}$.  
9. Set up the equation $T(\mathbf{b}_2) = d_1\mathbf{b}_1 + d_2\mathbf{b}_2$ or $\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix} = d_1\begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} + d_2\begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix}$, which gives the augmented matrix:  
\[
\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}
1 & 5 & 9 \\
1 & 4 & 7 \\
\end{array}\right]
\]  
10. Subtract row 1 from row 2: $R_2 \to R_2 - R_1$ gives  
\[
\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}
1 & 5 & 9 \\
0 & -1 & -2 \\
\end{array}\right]
\]  
11. Multiply row 2 by $-1$: $R_2 \to -R_2$ gives  
\[
\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}
1 & 5 & 9 \\
0 & 1 & 2 \\
\end{array}\right]
\]  
12. Subtract $5 \times$ row 2 from row 1: $R_1 \to R_1 - 5R_2$ gives  
\[
\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}
1 & 0 & -1 \\
0 & 1 & 2 \\
\end{array}\right]
\]  
13. Thus, $[T(\mathbf{b}_2)]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix}-1 \\ 2\end{bmatrix}$.  
14. Therefore, the $\mathcal{B}$-matrix for $T$ is $[T]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix}2 & -1 \\ 0 & 2\end{bmatrix}$.
