1. Let the unknown matrix be \(\begin{bmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ g & h & i \end{bmatrix}\), then multiplying by \(\begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \end{bmatrix}\) gives \(\begin{bmatrix} a x_1 + b x_2 + c x_3 \\ d x_1 + e x_2 + f x_3 \\ g x_1 + h x_2 + i x_3 \end{bmatrix}\).
2. Set the first row equal: \(a x_1 + b x_2 + c x_3 = 3x_1 - 2x_3\), so \(a = 3\), \(b = 0\), \(c = -2\).
3. Set the second row equal: \(d x_1 + e x_2 + f x_3 = 4x_1\), so \(d = 4\), \(e = 0\), \(f = 0\).
4. Set the third row equal: \(g x_1 + h x_2 + i x_3 = x_1 - x_2 + x_3\), so \(g = 1\), \(h = -1\), \(i = 1\).
5. The completed matrix is \(\begin{bmatrix} 3 & 0 & -2 \\ 4 & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\).
