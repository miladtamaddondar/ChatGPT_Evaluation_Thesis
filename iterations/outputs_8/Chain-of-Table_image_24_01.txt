1. Write the augmented matrix for the homogeneous system \(A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}\): \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 & 0 \end{array}\right]\)
2. Row 3 minus 2 times Row 1: \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -8 & 6 & 0 \end{array}\right]\)
3. Row 3 minus 2 times Row 2: \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\)
4. The reduced system is: \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\)
5. Write the equations: \(x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0\), \(x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0\)
6. Express \(x_2\) in terms of \(x_3, x_4\): \(x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4\)
7. Substitute \(x_2\) into the first equation: \(x_1 - 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0\)
8. Simplify: \(x_1 - 16x_3 + 12x_4 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0\) so \(x_1 - 9x_3 + 7x_4 = 0\)
9. Solve for \(x_1\): \(x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4\)
10. The general solution is: \(\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{bmatrix} = x_3 \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + x_4 \begin{bmatrix} -7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\), where \(x_3, x_4 \in \mathbb{R}\)
