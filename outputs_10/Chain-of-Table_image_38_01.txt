1. Write the basis \(\mathcal{B}\) and the target polynomial \(p(t)\): \(\mathcal{B} = \{1,\, 2t,\, -2 + 4t^2,\, -12t + 8t^3\}\), \(p(t) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3\).  
2. Set up the equation \(c_1(1) + c_2(2t) + c_3(-2 + 4t^2) + c_4(-12t + 8t^3) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3\).  
3. Expand and collect like terms: \(c_1 + 2c_2 t + (-2c_3 + 4c_3 t^2) + (-12c_4 t + 8c_4 t^3) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3\).  
4. Group by powers of \(t\): Constant: \(c_1 - 2c_3\), \(t\): \(2c_2 - 12c_4\), \(t^2\): \(4c_3\), \(t^3\): \(8c_4\).  
5. Set up the system: \(c_1 - 2c_3 = 7\), \(2c_2 - 12c_4 = -12\), \(4c_3 = -8\), \(8c_4 = 12\).  
6. Write the augmented matrix: \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 & | & 7 \\ 0 & 2 & 0 & -12 & | & -12 \\ 0 & 0 & 4 & 0 & | & -8 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 8 & | & 12 \end{bmatrix}\).  
7. Solve the fourth equation: \(8c_4 = 12 \implies c_4 = \frac{12}{8} = \frac{3}{2}\).  
8. Solve the third equation: \(4c_3 = -8 \implies c_3 = \frac{-8}{4} = -2\).  
9. Solve the second equation: \(2c_2 - 12c_4 = -12 \implies 2c_2 - 12(\frac{3}{2}) = -12 \implies 2c_2 - 18 = -12 \implies 2c_2 = 6 \implies c_2 = 3\).  
10. Solve the first equation: \(c_1 - 2c_3 = 7 \implies c_1 - 2(-2) = 7 \implies c_1 + 4 = 7 \implies c_1 = 3\).  
11. The coordinate vector of \(p\) relative to \(\mathcal{B}\) is \(\boxed{\begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{bmatrix}}\).
