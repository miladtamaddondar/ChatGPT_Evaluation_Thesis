11. Row 2 $\to$ Row 2 + 7/18(Row 3): $\begin{bmatrix}1 & -3 & 2 & -4 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 17/18 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.

15. Basis for $\text{Col }A$: The pivot columns of $A$ are columns 1, 3, and 4, so a basis is $\left\{ \begin{bmatrix}1\\3\\2\\-4\end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix}2\\-1\\4\\2\end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix}-4\\5\\-3\\7\end{bmatrix} \right\}$.

17. Dimensions: $\dim(\text{Col }A) = 3$, $\dim(\text{Nul }A) = 1$.
