2. Expand the determinant: $(0-\lambda)\begin{vmatrix}2-\lambda & 5 \\ 0 & 1-\lambda\end{vmatrix} = (0-\lambda)\left((2-\lambda)(1-\lambda)-0\cdot5\right) = -\lambda(2-\lambda)(1-\lambda)$.
3. Set the characteristic polynomial to zero: $-\lambda(2-\lambda)(1-\lambda) = 0$.
4. Solve for $\lambda$: $\lambda = 0,\ 1,\ 2$.
5. Final answer: The eigenvalues are $\boxed{0,\ 1,\ 2}$.
