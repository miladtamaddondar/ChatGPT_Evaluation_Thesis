1. Write each basis vector in \(\mathcal{B}\) in terms of the standard basis \(\{1, t, t^2\}\): \(1 - 3t^2 = 1 \cdot 1 + 0 \cdot t + (-3) \cdot t^2\), \(2 + t - 5t^2 = 2 \cdot 1 + 1 \cdot t + (-5) \cdot t^2\), \(1 + 2t = 1 \cdot 1 + 2 \cdot t + 0 \cdot t^2\).
2. Form the change-of-coordinates matrix \(P\) by writing these as columns: \[ P = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 \\ -3 & -5 & 0 \end{bmatrix} \].
3. To write \(t^2\) as a linear combination of the polynomials in \(\mathcal{B}\), solve \(c_1(1 - 3t^2) + c_2(2 + t - 5t^2) + c_3(1 + 2t) = t^2\).
4. Express as a system: \(c_1(1) + c_2(2) + c_3(1) = 0\), \(c_1(0) + c_2(1) + c_3(2) = 0\), \(c_1(-3) + c_2(-5) + c_3(0) = 1\).
5. Write the augmented matrix: \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 0 \\ -3 & -5 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\).
6. Row 3 + 3 × Row 1: \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 3 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\).
7. Row 3 - Row 2: \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\).
8. Row 2 - 2 × Row 3: \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\).
9. Row 1 - Row 3: \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 0 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\).
10. Row 1 - 2 × Row 2: \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\).
11. Solution: \(c_1 = 3\), \(c_2 = -2\), \(c_3 = 1\), so \(t^2 = 3(1 - 3t^2) - 2(2 + t - 5t^2) + 1(1 + 2t)\) as a linear combination of the polynomials in \(\mathcal{B}\).
