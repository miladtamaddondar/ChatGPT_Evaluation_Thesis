1. Write the equation \( p(t) = a(1+t^2) + b(t+t^2) + c(1+2t+t^2) \), so \( 1 + 4t + 7t^2 = a(1+t^2) + b(t+t^2) + c(1+2t+t^2) \). Expanding and collecting like terms gives \( (a+c) + (b+2c)t + (a+b+c)t^2 = 1 + 4t + 7t^2 \).
2. Set up the system of equations: \( a + c = 1 \), \( b + 2c = 4 \), \( a + b + c = 7 \). Write the augmented matrix: \( \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 4 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & 7 \end{array}\right] \).
3. Subtract row 1 from row 3: \( R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - R_1 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 4 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 6 \end{array}\right] \).
4. Subtract row 2 from row 3: \( R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - R_2 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 4 \\ 0 & 0 & -2 & 2 \end{array}\right] \).
5. Divide row 3 by \(-2\): \( R_3 \leftarrow \frac{1}{-2}R_3 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 2 & 4 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 \end{array}\right] \).
6. Subtract \(2 \times R_3\) from row 2: \( R_2 \leftarrow R_2 - 2R_3 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 6 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 \end{array}\right] \).
7. Subtract row 3 from row 1: \( R_1 \leftarrow R_1 - R_3 \): \( \left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 6 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & -1 \end{array}\right] \).
8. The solution is \( \boxed{\begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 6 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix}} \), so the coordinate vector of \( p(t) \) relative to \( \mathcal{B} \) is \( \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 6 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix} \).
