1. Let the columns of the matrix be \( \mathbf{a}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \), \( \mathbf{a}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \), \( \mathbf{a}_3 = \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ 7 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \).
2. Set \( \mathbf{v}_1 = \mathbf{a}_1 \) as the first vector in the orthogonal basis.
3. Compute the projection of \( \mathbf{a}_2 \) onto \( \mathbf{v}_1 \): \( \text{proj}_{\mathbf{v}_1} \mathbf{a}_2 = \frac{\mathbf{a}_2 \cdot \mathbf{v}_1}{\mathbf{v}_1 \cdot \mathbf{v}_1} \mathbf{v}_1 = \frac{2 - 1 - 4 - 4 + 2}{1 + 1 + 1 + 1 + 1} \mathbf{v}_1 = \frac{-5}{5} \mathbf{v}_1 = -\mathbf{v}_1 \).
4. Subtract the projection from \( \mathbf{a}_2 \) to get \( \mathbf{v}_2 \): \( \mathbf{v}_2 = \mathbf{a}_2 - (-\mathbf{v}_1) = \mathbf{a}_2 + \mathbf{v}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix} \).
5. Compute the projection of \( \mathbf{a}_3 \) onto \( \mathbf{v}_1 \): \( \text{proj}_{\mathbf{v}_1} \mathbf{a}_3 = \frac{5 + 4 + 3 + 7 + 1}{5} \mathbf{v}_1 = \frac{20}{5} \mathbf{v}_1 = 4\mathbf{v}_1 \).
6. Compute the projection of \( \mathbf{a}_3 \) onto \( \mathbf{v}_2 \): \( \text{proj}_{\mathbf{v}_2} \mathbf{a}_3 = \frac{5 \cdot 3 + (-4) \cdot 0 + (-3) \cdot 3 + 7 \cdot (-3) + 1 \cdot 3}{3^2 + 0^2 + 3^2 + (-3)^2 + 3^2} \mathbf{v}_2 = \frac{15 + 0 - 9 - 21 + 3}{9 + 0 + 9 + 9 + 9} \mathbf{v}_2 = \frac{-12}{36} \mathbf{v}_2 = -\frac{1}{3} \mathbf{v}_2 \).
7. Subtract both projections from \( \mathbf{a}_3 \) to get \( \mathbf{v}_3 \): \( \mathbf{v}_3 = \mathbf{a}_3 - 4\mathbf{v}_1 + \frac{1}{3}\mathbf{v}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ 7 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} - 4\begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} + \frac{1}{3}\begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 5-4+1 \\ -4+4+0 \\ -3+4+1 \\ 7-4-1 \\ 1-4+1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ -2 \end{bmatrix} \).
8. The orthogonal basis for the column space is \( \left\{ \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ -2 \end{bmatrix} \right\} \).
