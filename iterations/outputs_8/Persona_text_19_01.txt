5. Solve $9\mathbf{e}_1 + 7\mathbf{e}_2 = x\mathbf{b}_1 + y\mathbf{b}_2$; set up $x\begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} + y\begin{bmatrix}5 \\ 4\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 7\end{bmatrix}$.  
6. This gives the system: $x + 5y = 9$, $x + 4y = 7$.  
7. Subtract the second equation from the first: $(x + 5y) - (x + 4y) = 9 - 7 \implies y = 2$.  
8. Substitute $y = 2$ into $x + 5y = 9$: $x + 10 = 9 \implies x = -1$.
