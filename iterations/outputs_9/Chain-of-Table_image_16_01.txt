4. Compute \( T(t^2) \): \( T(t^2) = T(0 + 0t + 1t^2) = 3(0) + (5(0) - 2(0))t + (4(0) + 1)t^2 = t^2 \). So, as a vector: \( [T(t^2)]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \).
