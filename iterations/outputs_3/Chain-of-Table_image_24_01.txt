1. Write the augmented matrix for the homogeneous system \(A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}\): \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).  
2. Subtract 2 times row 1 from row 3: \(R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 2R_1\): \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -8 & 6 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).  
3. Subtract 2 times row 2 from row 3: \(R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 2R_2\): \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).  
4. Write the system from the row-reduced matrix: \(x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0\), \(x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0\).  
5. Express leading variables in terms of free variables \(x_3 = s, x_4 = t\): \(x_2 = 4s - 3t\), \(x_1 = 9s - 7t\).  
6. Write the general solution: \(\mathbf{x} = s\begin{bmatrix}9\\4\\1\\0\end{bmatrix} + t\begin{bmatrix}-7\\-3\\0\\1\end{bmatrix}\), where \(s, t \in \mathbb{R}\).
