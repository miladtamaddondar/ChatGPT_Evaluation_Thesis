1. The problem asks for the matrix of the linear transformation \( T(\mathbf{x}) = A\mathbf{x} \) with respect to the basis \( \mathcal{B} = \{\mathbf{b}_1, \mathbf{b}_2\} \), where \( \mathbf{b}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \) and \( \mathbf{b}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} \).  
2. To find the \( \mathcal{B} \)-matrix, we need to compute \( [T]_{\mathcal{B}} \), whose columns are the coordinates of \( T(\mathbf{b}_1) \) and \( T(\mathbf{b}_2) \) in the basis \( \mathcal{B} \).  
3. Compute \( T(\mathbf{b}_1) = A\mathbf{b}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \).  
4. Compute \( T(\mathbf{b}_2) = A\mathbf{b}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} \).  
5. Express \( \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \) as a linear combination of \( \mathbf{b}_1 \) and \( \mathbf{b}_2 \): set \( \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} = c_1 \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} + c_2 \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} \), so \( \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} c_1 + 5c_2 \\ c_1 + 4c_2 \end{bmatrix} \).  
6. Solve for \( c_1 \) and \( c_2 \): \( c_1 + 5c_2 = 2 \) and \( c_1 + 4c_2 = 2 \) gives \( c_2 = 0 \), \( c_1 = 2 \), so \( [T(\mathbf{b}_1)]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \).  
7. Express \( \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} \) as a linear combination of \( \mathbf{b}_1 \) and \( \mathbf{b}_2 \): set \( \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} = d_1 \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} + d_2 \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} \), so \( \begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} d_1 + 5d_2 \\ d_1 + 4d_2 \end{bmatrix} \).  
8. Solve for \( d_1 \) and \( d_2 \): \( d_1 + 5d_2 = 9 \) and \( d_1 + 4d_2 = 7 \) gives \( d_2 = 2 \), \( d_1 = -1 \), so \( [T(\mathbf{b}_2)]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \).  
9. The \( \mathcal{B} \)-matrix for \( T \) is \( [T]_{\mathcal{B}} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \).
