1. Subtract \(4I\) from \(A\) to form \(A - 4I\): \(\begin{bmatrix} 3-4 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 1 & 3-4 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 1-4 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 4-4 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}\).
2. Set up the augmented matrix for \((A-4I)\vec{x} = \vec{0}\): \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).
3. Add row 1 to row 2: \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).
4. Add row 2 to row 3: \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} -1 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).
5. Multiply row 1 by \(-1\) and row 2 by \(-1\): \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 & 0 & -2 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\).
6. Write the system: \(x_1 - 2x_3 = 0\), \(x_2 - 3x_3 = 0\), \(x_4\) is free.
7. Express the general solution: \(x_1 = 2x_3\), \(x_2 = 3x_3\), \(x_3 = x_3\), \(x_4 = t\).
8. Write the vector form: \(\vec{x} = x_3\begin{bmatrix}2\\3\\1\\0\end{bmatrix} + t\begin{bmatrix}0\\0\\0\\1\end{bmatrix}\).
9. The basis for the eigenspace is \(\left\{\begin{bmatrix}2\\3\\1\\0\end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix}0\\0\\0\\1\end{bmatrix}\right\}\).
