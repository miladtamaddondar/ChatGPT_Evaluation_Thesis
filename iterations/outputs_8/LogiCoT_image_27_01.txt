1. Since \( T \) maps \( \mathbf{e}_1 \) to \( \mathbf{y}_1 \) and \( \mathbf{e}_2 \) to \( \mathbf{y}_2 \), the standard matrix of \( T \) is \( A = [\mathbf{y}_1 \ \mathbf{y}_2] = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \).  
2. To find the image of \( \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} \), compute \( A \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} = 5\mathbf{y}_1 + (-3)\mathbf{y}_2 = 5\begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} - 3\begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix} \).  
3. Calculate \( 5\begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 10 \\ 25 \end{bmatrix} \) and \( -3\begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ -18 \end{bmatrix} \), then add: \( \begin{bmatrix} 10 \\ 25 \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ -18 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 13 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} \).  
4. Verify by direct matrix multiplication: \( \begin{bmatrix} 2 & -1 \\ 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \cdot 5 + (-1) \cdot (-3) \\ 5 \cdot 5 + 6 \cdot (-3) \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 10 + 3 \\ 25 - 18 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 13 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} \), confirming correctness.  
5. To find the image of \( \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} \), compute \( A\begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} = x_1\mathbf{y}_1 + x_2\mathbf{y}_2 = x_1\begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} + x_2\begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix} \).  
6. This gives \( \begin{bmatrix} 2x_1 - x_2 \\ 5x_1 + 6x_2 \end{bmatrix} \) as the image of \( \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} \) under \( T \).  
7. Final answers: The image of \( \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} \) is \( \begin{bmatrix} 13 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} \), and the image of \( \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix} \) is \( \begin{bmatrix} 2x_1 - x_2 \\ 5x_1 + 6x_2 \end{bmatrix} \).
