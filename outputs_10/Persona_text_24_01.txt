1. To find all $\mathbf{x}$ in $\mathbb{R}^4$ mapped to the zero vector by $A$, we solve $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$, i.e., we find the null space (kernel) of $A$.  
2. Write the system $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$ as: $\begin{cases} x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0 \\ x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0 \\ 2x_1 - 6x_2 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0 \end{cases}$.  
3. Write the augmented matrix: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.  
4. Subtract $2$ times row 1 from row 3: Row 3 $\to$ Row 3 $- 2 \times$ Row 1: $[2, -6, 6, -4, 0] - 2 \times [1, -4, 7, -5, 0] = [0, 2, -8, 6, 0]$.  
5. The matrix is now $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -8 & 6 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.  
6. Subtract $2$ times row 2 from row 3: Row 3 $\to$ Row 3 $- 2 \times$ Row 2: $[0, 2, -8, 6, 0] - 2 \times [0, 1, -4, 3, 0] = [0, 0, 0, 0, 0]$.  
7. The matrix is now $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.  
8. Write the system from the reduced matrix: $\begin{cases} x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0 \\ x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0 \end{cases}$.  
9. Express $x_2$ in terms of $x_3$ and $x_4$: $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$.  
10. Substitute $x_2$ into the first equation: $x_1 - 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0 \implies x_1 - 16x_3 + 12x_4 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.  
11. Simplify: $x_1 - 9x_3 + 7x_4 = 0 \implies x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4$.  
12. Let $x_3 = s$ and $x_4 = t$ for $s, t \in \mathbb{R}$, so $x_2 = 4s - 3t$ and $x_1 = 9s - 7t$.  
13. The general solution is $\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 9s - 7t \\ 4s - 3t \\ s \\ t \end{bmatrix} = s\begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + t\begin{bmatrix} -7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}$.  
14. Final answer: All $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^4$ mapped to zero by $A$ are $\boxed{\mathbf{x} = s\begin{bmatrix} 9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + t\begin{bmatrix} -7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix},\quad s, t \in \mathbb{R}}$.
