1. Find \(T(1)\): \(T(1) = T(1 + 0t + 0t^2) = 3(1) + (5 \cdot 1 - 2 \cdot 0)t + (4 \cdot 0 + 0)t^2 = 3 + 5t\).  
2. Find \(T(t)\): \(T(t) = T(0 + 1t + 0t^2) = 3(0) + (5 \cdot 0 - 2 \cdot 1)t + (4 \cdot 1 + 0)t^2 = -2t + 4t^2\).  
3. Find \(T(t^2)\): \(T(t^2) = T(0 + 0t + 1t^2) = 3(0) + (5 \cdot 0 - 2 \cdot 0)t + (4 \cdot 0 + 1)t^2 = t^2\).  
4. Express each result in coordinates relative to \(\mathcal{B} = \{1, t, t^2\}\): \(T(1) = 3 \cdot 1 + 5 \cdot t + 0 \cdot t^2 \rightarrow \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 5 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}\), \(T(t) = 0 \cdot 1 + (-2) \cdot t + 4 \cdot t^2 \rightarrow \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ -2 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix}\), \(T(t^2) = 0 \cdot 1 + 0 \cdot t + 1 \cdot t^2 \rightarrow \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\).  
5. Write the matrix representation: The matrix of \(T\) relative to \(\mathcal{B}\) is \(\begin{bmatrix} 3 & 0 & 0 \\ 5 & -2 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\).
