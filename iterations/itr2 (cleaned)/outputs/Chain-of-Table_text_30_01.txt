1. Write the system of equations for $T(x_1, x_2) = (-1, 4, 9)$: $x_1 - 2x_2 = -1$, $-x_1 + 3x_2 = 4$, $3x_1 - 2x_2 = 9$.
2. Write the augmented matrix: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 & | & -1 \\ -1 & 3 & | & 4 \\ 3 & -2 & | & 9 \end{bmatrix}$.
3. Apply $R_2 \leftarrow R_2 + R_1$: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 & | & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 3 & -2 & | & 9 \end{bmatrix}$.
4. Apply $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 3R_1$: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 & | & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & 4 & | & 12 \end{bmatrix}$.
5. Apply $R_3 \leftarrow R_3 - 4R_2$: $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -2 & | & -1 \\ 0 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & | & 0 \end{bmatrix}$.
6. Back-substitute: From $R_2$, $x_2 = 3$; from $R_1$, $x_1 - 2(3) = -1 \implies x_1 = 5$.
7. Final answer: $\boxed{\mathbf{x} = \begin{pmatrix} 5 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix}}$.
