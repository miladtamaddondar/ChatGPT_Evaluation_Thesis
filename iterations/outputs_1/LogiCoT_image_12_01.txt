1. Write the matrix \( A = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 1 \\ -1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \). 
2. The characteristic polynomial is found by computing \( \det(A - \lambda I) \). 
3. Compute \( A - \lambda I = \begin{bmatrix} 2-\lambda & 1 \\ -1 & 4-\lambda \end{bmatrix} \). 
4. The determinant is \( (2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) - (-1)(1) \). 
5. Expand: \( (2-\lambda)(4-\lambda) + 1 = 8 - 2\lambda - 4\lambda + \lambda^2 + 1 \).
6. Simplify: \( \lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9 \). 
7. The characteristic polynomial is \( \lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9 \). 
8. Set the polynomial to zero: \( \lambda^2 - 6\lambda + 9 = 0 \). 
9. Factor: \( (\lambda - 3)^2 = 0 \). 
10. The eigenvalues are \( \lambda_1 = 3 \) and \( \lambda_2 = 3 \).
