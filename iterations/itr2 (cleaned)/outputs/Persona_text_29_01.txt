1. To find $\mathbf{x} = (x_1, x_2)$ such that $T(\mathbf{x}) = (3, 8)$, set up the system: $T(x_1, x_2) = (x_1 + x_2, 4x_1 + 5x_2) = (3, 8)$.  
2. Equate components to get two equations: $x_1 + x_2 = 3$ and $4x_1 + 5x_2 = 8$.  
3. Solve the first equation for $x_1$: $x_1 = 3 - x_2$.  
4. Substitute $x_1$ into the second equation: $4(3 - x_2) + 5x_2 = 8$.  
5. Expand and simplify: $12 - 4x_2 + 5x_2 = 8 \implies 12 + x_2 = 8$.  
6. Solve for $x_2$: $x_2 = 8 - 12 = -4$.  
7. Substitute $x_2 = -4$ back into $x_1 = 3 - x_2$: $x_1 = 3 - (-4) = 7$.  
8. Thus, the solution is $\mathbf{x} = (7, -4)$.
