1. The transformation $T(\mathbf{x}) = x_1\mathbf{v}_1 + x_2\mathbf{v}_2$ can be written as $T(\mathbf{x}) = x_1\begin{bmatrix} -2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix} + x_2\begin{bmatrix} 7 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -2x_1 + 7x_2 \\ 5x_1 - 3x_2 \end{bmatrix}$.
2. To express $T(\mathbf{x})$ as $A\mathbf{x}$, write $A$ so that $A\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix} -2 & 7 \\ 5 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \end{bmatrix}$.
3. Therefore, the answer is: $A = \begin{bmatrix} -2 & 7 \\ 5 & -3 \end{bmatrix}$.
