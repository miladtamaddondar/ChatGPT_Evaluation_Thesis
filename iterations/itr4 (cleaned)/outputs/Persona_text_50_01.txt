### Part (a): Orthogonal Projection of $p_2$ onto $\text{Span}\{p_0, p_1\}$
1. The inner product is defined as $\langle f, g \rangle = f(-3)g(-3) + f(-1)g(-1) + f(1)g(1) + f(3)g(3)$.  
2. The orthogonal projection of $p_2$ onto $\text{Span}\{p_0, p_1\}$ is $\operatorname{proj}_W(p_2) = a_0 p_0 + a_1 p_1$, where $a_0 = \frac{\langle p_2, p_0 \rangle}{\langle p_0, p_0 \rangle}$ and $a_1 = \frac{\langle p_2, p_1 \rangle}{\langle p_1, p_1 \rangle}$.  
3. Compute $p_0(t) = 1$, so $p_0(-3) = p_0(-1) = p_0(1) = p_0(3) = 1$.  
4. Compute $p_1(t) = t$, so $p_1(-3) = -3$, $p_1(-1) = -1$, $p_1(1) = 1$, $p_1(3) = 3$.  
5. Compute $p_2(t) = t^2$, so $p_2(-3) = 9$, $p_2(-1) = 1$, $p_2(1) = 1$, $p_2(3) = 9$.  
6. Compute $\langle p_2, p_0 \rangle = 9 \cdot 1 + 1 \cdot 1 + 1 \cdot 1 + 9 \cdot 1 = 9 + 1 + 1 + 9 = 20$.  
7. Compute $\langle p_0, p_0 \rangle = 1^2 + 1^2 + 1^2 + 1^2 = 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 = 4$.  
8. Compute $a_0 = \frac{20}{4} = 5$.  
9. Compute $\langle p_2, p_1 \rangle = 9 \cdot (-3) + 1 \cdot (-1) + 1 \cdot 1 + 9 \cdot 3 = -27 - 1 + 1 + 27 = 0$.  
10. Compute $\langle p_1, p_1 \rangle = (-3)^2 + (-1)^2 + 1^2 + 3^2 = 9 + 1 + 1 + 9 = 20$.  
11. Compute $a_1 = \frac{0}{20} = 0$.  
12. Therefore, the orthogonal projection is $\operatorname{proj}_W(p_2) = 5 \cdot p_0 + 0 \cdot p_1 = 5$.  
### Part (b): Find $q$ orthogonal to $p_0$ and $p_1$ and scale as specified
13. Let $q(t) = p_2(t) - \operatorname{proj}_W(p_2) = t^2 - 5$.  
14. Check orthogonality: $\langle q, p_0 \rangle = \langle t^2 - 5, 1 \rangle = \langle t^2, 1 \rangle - 5 \langle 1, 1 \rangle = 20 - 5 \cdot 4 = 0$.  
15. Check orthogonality: $\langle q, p_1 \rangle = \langle t^2 - 5, t \rangle = \langle t^2, t \rangle - 5 \langle 1, t \rangle = 0 - 5 \cdot 0 = 0$.  
16. Evaluate $q$ at $(-3, -1, 1, 3)$: $q(-3) = 9 - 5 = 4$, $q(-1) = 1 - 5 = -4$, $q(1) = 1 - 5 = -4$, $q(3) = 9 - 5 = 4$.  
17. To scale $q$ so its vector of values is $(1, -1, -1, 1)$, set $q_{\text{scaled}}(t) = \frac{1}{4}(t^2 - 5)$.  
18. Therefore, the required polynomial is $q(t) = \frac{1}{4}(t^2 - 5)$.
