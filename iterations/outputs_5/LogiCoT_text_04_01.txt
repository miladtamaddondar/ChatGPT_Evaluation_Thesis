1. Compute \( AD \) by multiplying matrix \( A \) with diagonal matrix \( D \): \( AD = \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 1 & 4 & 5\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}2 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 5\end{bmatrix} \); since \( D \) is diagonal, each column of \( A \) gets multiplied by the corresponding diagonal entry of \( D \).
2. Calculate each entry of \( AD \): first row: \( (1\cdot2 + 1\cdot0 + 1\cdot0, 1\cdot0 + 1\cdot3 + 1\cdot0, 1\cdot0 + 1\cdot0 + 1\cdot5) = (2, 3, 5) \); second row: \( (1\cdot2 + 2\cdot0 + 3\cdot0, 1\cdot0 + 2\cdot3 + 3\cdot0, 1\cdot0 + 2\cdot0 + 3\cdot5) = (2, 6, 15) \); third row: \( (1\cdot2 + 4\cdot0 + 5\cdot0, 1\cdot0 + 4\cdot3 + 5\cdot0, 1\cdot0 + 4\cdot0 + 5\cdot5) = (2, 12, 25) \).
3. Thus, \( AD = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 3 & 5 \\ 2 & 6 & 15 \\ 2 & 12 & 25\end{bmatrix} \); this is correct because multiplying by a diagonal matrix on the right scales the columns of \( A \) by the diagonal entries of \( D \).
4. Compute \( DA \) by multiplying diagonal matrix \( D \) with matrix \( A \): \( DA = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 5\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 1 & 4 & 5\end{bmatrix} \); since \( D \) is diagonal, each row of \( A \) gets multiplied by the corresponding diagonal entry of \( D \).
5. Calculate each entry of \( DA \): first row: \( (2\cdot1 + 0\cdot1 + 0\cdot1, 2\cdot1 + 0\cdot2 + 0\cdot4, 2\cdot1 + 0\cdot3 + 0\cdot5) = (2, 2, 2) \); second row: \( (0\cdot1 + 3\cdot1 + 0\cdot1, 0\cdot1 + 3\cdot2 + 0\cdot4, 0\cdot1 + 3\cdot3 + 0\cdot5) = (3, 6, 9) \); third row: \( (0\cdot1 + 0\cdot1 + 5\cdot1, 0\cdot1 + 0\cdot2 + 5\cdot4, 0\cdot1 + 0\cdot3 + 5\cdot5) = (5, 20, 25) \).
6. Thus, \( DA = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 2 & 2 \\ 3 & 6 & 9 \\ 5 & 20 & 25\end{bmatrix} \); this is correct because multiplying by a diagonal matrix on the left scales the rows of \( A \) by the diagonal entries of \( D \).
7. Final answers: \( AD = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 3 & 5 \\ 2 & 6 & 15 \\ 2 & 12 & 25\end{bmatrix} \) and \( DA = \begin{bmatrix}2 & 2 & 2 \\ 3 & 6 & 9 \\ 5 & 20 & 25\end{bmatrix} \).
