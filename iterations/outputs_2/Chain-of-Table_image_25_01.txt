4. Match coefficients for the first row: \(a = 3\), \(b = 0\), \(c = -2\). 
5. Match coefficients for the second row: \(d = 4\), \(e = 0\), \(f = 0\). 
6. Match coefficients for the third row: \(g = 1\), \(h = -1\), \(i = 1\).
