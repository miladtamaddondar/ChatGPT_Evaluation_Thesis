1. Write the basis \(\mathcal{B}\) as \(\{1,\, 2t,\, -2+4t^2,\, -12t+8t^3\}\).
2. Express \(p(t) = 7 - 12t - 8t^2 + 12t^3\) as a linear combination: \(p(t) = a\cdot 1 + b\cdot 2t + c\cdot (-2+4t^2) + d\cdot (-12t+8t^3)\).
3. Expand the combination: \(p(t) = a + 2b t - 2c + 4c t^2 - 12d t + 8d t^3\).
4. Collect like terms: \(p(t) = (a-2c) + (2b-12d)t + 4c t^2 + 8d t^3\).
5. Set up equations by matching coefficients: \(a-2c = 7\), \(2b-12d = -12\), \(4c = -8\), \(8d = 12\).
6. Solve \(4c = -8 \implies c = -2\).
7. Solve \(8d = 12 \implies d = \frac{3}{2}\).
8. Substitute \(c = -2\) into \(a-2c=7\): \(a-2(-2)=7 \implies a+4=7 \implies a=3\).
9. Substitute \(d = \frac{3}{2}\) into \(2b-12d=-12\): \(2b-12(\frac{3}{2})=-12 \implies 2b-18=-12 \implies 2b=6 \implies b=3\).
10. The coordinate vector of \(p\) relative to \(\mathcal{B}\) is \(\begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 3 \\ -2 \\ \frac{3}{2} \end{bmatrix}\).
