1. Write the vectors as columns of a matrix \(A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & -3 & 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & -3 & 1 \\ -3 & 2 & 1 & -8 & -6 \\ 2 & -3 & 6 & 7 & 9 \end{bmatrix}\).
2. Begin row reducing \(A\) to row echelon form to find linearly independent columns.
3. The first column has a leading 1 in row 1, so keep column 1.
4. Subtract 0 times column 1 from column 2 (no change), so column 2 has a leading 1 in row 2, keep column 2.
5. Add 3 times column 1 to column 3: \([-3+3\cdot1, -4+3\cdot0, 1+3\cdot(-3), 6+3\cdot2] = [0, -4, -8, 12]\).
6. Add -1 times column 1 to column 4: \([1-1\cdot1, -3-1\cdot0, -8-1\cdot(-3), 7-1\cdot2] = [0, -3, -5, 5]\).
7. Add -2 times column 1 to column 5: \([2-2\cdot1, 1-2\cdot0, -6-2\cdot(-3), 9-2\cdot2] = [0, 1, 0, 5]\).
8. Now matrix is \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & -3 & 1 \\ -3 & 2 & -8 & -5 & 0 \\ 2 & -3 & 12 & 5 & 5 \end{bmatrix}\).
9. Subtract 2 times row 2 from row 3: \([-3-2\cdot0, 2-2\cdot1, -8-2\cdot(-4), -5-2\cdot(-3), 0-2\cdot1] = [-3, 0, 0, 1, -2]\).
10. Subtract -3 times row 2 from row 4: \([2-(-3)\cdot0, -3-(-3)\cdot1, 12-(-3)\cdot(-4), 5-(-3)\cdot(-3), 5-(-3)\cdot1] = [2, 0, 0, -4, 8]\).
11. Now matrix is \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & -3 & 1 \\ -3 & 0 & 0 & 1 & -2 \\ 2 & 0 & 0 & -4 & 8 \end{bmatrix}\).
12. Add 3 times row 1 to row 3: \([-3+3\cdot1, 0+3\cdot0, 0+3\cdot0, 1+3\cdot0, -2+3\cdot0] = [0, 0, 0, 1, -2]\).
13. Subtract 2 times row 1 from row 4: \([2-2\cdot1, 0-2\cdot0, 0-2\cdot0, -4-2\cdot0, 8-2\cdot0] = [0, 0, 0, -4, 8]\).
14. Now matrix is \(\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & -3 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & -4 & 8 \end{bmatrix}\).
15. Add 4 times row 3 to row 4: \([0+4\cdot0, 0+4\cdot0, 0+4\cdot0, -4+4\cdot1, 8+4\cdot(-2)] = [0, 0, 0, 0, 0]\).
16. The row-reduced matrix has pivots in columns 1, 2, and 4, so columns 1, 2, and 4 of the original matrix form a basis.
17. The basis for the space spanned by the vectors is \(\left\{ \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \\ -3 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ -3 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -3 \\ -8 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} \right\}\).
