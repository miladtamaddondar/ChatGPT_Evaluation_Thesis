1. The standard matrix for a rotation by \(\theta\) radians counterclockwise is \(\begin{bmatrix} \cos\theta & -\sin\theta \\ \sin\theta & \cos\theta \end{bmatrix}\). 
2. Substitute \(\theta = \frac{3\pi}{2}\): \(\cos\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) = 0\), \(\sin\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) = -1\). 
3. Plug in the values: \(\begin{bmatrix} 0 & -(-1) \\ -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}\). 
4. The standard matrix of \(T\) is \(\boxed{\begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{bmatrix}}\).
