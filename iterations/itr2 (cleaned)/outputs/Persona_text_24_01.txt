1. We are asked to find all vectors $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^4$ such that $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$, which means we need to solve the homogeneous system $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$ where $A = \begin{bmatrix}1 & -4 & 7 & -5 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 \end{bmatrix}$.
2. Let $\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4\end{bmatrix}$, so the system is: $\begin{cases} 1x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0 \\ 0x_1 + 1x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0 \\ 2x_1 - 6x_2 + 6x_3 - 4x_4 = 0 \end{cases}$.
3. The augmented matrix is $\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 2 & -6 & 6 & -4 & 0 \end{array}\right]$.
4. Subtract $2$ times row 1 from row 3: Row 3 becomes $[2 - 2\cdot1, -6 - 2\cdot(-4), 6 - 2\cdot7, -4 - 2\cdot(-5)] = [0, 2, -8, 6]$.
5. The new matrix is $\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 & -8 & 6 & 0 \end{array}\right]$.
6. Subtract $2$ times row 2 from row 3: Row 3 becomes $[0, 2 - 2\cdot1, -8 - 2\cdot(-4), 6 - 2\cdot3] = [0, 0, 0, 0]$.
7. The reduced matrix is $\left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} 1 & -4 & 7 & -5 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & -4 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]$.
8. Write the equations from the reduced matrix: $x_1 - 4x_2 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$, $x_2 - 4x_3 + 3x_4 = 0$.
9. Express $x_2$ in terms of $x_3$ and $x_4$: $x_2 = 4x_3 - 3x_4$.
10. Substitute $x_2$ into the first equation: $x_1 - 4(4x_3 - 3x_4) + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.
11. Simplify: $x_1 - 16x_3 + 12x_4 + 7x_3 - 5x_4 = 0$.
12. Combine like terms: $x_1 + (-16x_3 + 7x_3) + (12x_4 - 5x_4) = 0 \implies x_1 - 9x_3 + 7x_4 = 0$.
13. Express $x_1$ in terms of $x_3$ and $x_4$: $x_1 = 9x_3 - 7x_4$.
14. Let $x_3 = s$ and $x_4 = t$ where $s, t \in \mathbb{R}$, then $x_2 = 4s - 3t$ and $x_1 = 9s - 7t$.
15. Therefore, the general solution is $\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}9s - 7t \\ 4s - 3t \\ s \\ t\end{bmatrix} = s\begin{bmatrix}9 \\ 4 \\ 1 \\ 0\end{bmatrix} + t\begin{bmatrix}-7 \\ -3 \\ 0 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}$ for $s, t \in \mathbb{R}$.
