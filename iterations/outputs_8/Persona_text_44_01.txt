1. Let the columns of the matrix be \( \mathbf{a}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \), \( \mathbf{a}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \), and \( \mathbf{a}_3 = \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ 7 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \).
2. Set \( \mathbf{u}_1 = \mathbf{a}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \).
3. Compute the projection of \( \mathbf{a}_2 \) onto \( \mathbf{u}_1 \): \( \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_1} \mathbf{a}_2 = \frac{\mathbf{a}_2 \cdot \mathbf{u}_1}{\mathbf{u}_1 \cdot \mathbf{u}_1} \mathbf{u}_1 \).
4. Calculate \( \mathbf{a}_2 \cdot \mathbf{u}_1 = 2 \cdot 1 + 1 \cdot (-1) + 4 \cdot (-1) + (-4) \cdot 1 + 2 \cdot 1 = 2 - 1 - 4 - 4 + 2 = -5 \).
5. Calculate \( \mathbf{u}_1 \cdot \mathbf{u}_1 = 1^2 + (-1)^2 + (-1)^2 + 1^2 + 1^2 = 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 = 5 \).
6. Thus, \( \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_1} \mathbf{a}_2 = \frac{-5}{5} \mathbf{u}_1 = -1 \cdot \mathbf{u}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix} \).
7. Compute \( \mathbf{u}_2 = \mathbf{a}_2 - \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_1} \mathbf{a}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 1 \\ 4 \\ -4 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix} \).
8. Compute the projection of \( \mathbf{a}_3 \) onto \( \mathbf{u}_1 \): \( \mathbf{a}_3 \cdot \mathbf{u}_1 = 5 \cdot 1 + (-4) \cdot (-1) + (-3) \cdot (-1) + 7 \cdot 1 + 1 \cdot 1 = 5 + 4 + 3 + 7 + 1 = 20 \).
9. So \( \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_1} \mathbf{a}_3 = \frac{20}{5} \mathbf{u}_1 = 4 \cdot \mathbf{u}_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ 4 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} \).
10. Compute the projection of \( \mathbf{a}_3 \) onto \( \mathbf{u}_2 \): \( \mathbf{a}_3 \cdot \mathbf{u}_2 = 5 \cdot 3 + (-4) \cdot 0 + (-3) \cdot 3 + 7 \cdot (-3) + 1 \cdot 3 = 15 + 0 - 9 - 21 + 3 = -12 \).
11. \( \mathbf{u}_2 \cdot \mathbf{u}_2 = 3^2 + 0^2 + 3^2 + (-3)^2 + 3^2 = 9 + 0 + 9 + 9 + 9 = 36 \).
12. So \( \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_2} \mathbf{a}_3 = \frac{-12}{36} \mathbf{u}_2 = -\frac{1}{3} \cdot \mathbf{u}_2 = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix} \).
13. Compute \( \mathbf{u}_3 = \mathbf{a}_3 - \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_1} \mathbf{a}_3 - \mathrm{proj}_{\mathbf{u}_2} \mathbf{a}_3 = \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ -4 \\ -3 \\ 7 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ -4 \\ -4 \\ 4 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 0 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ -2 \end{bmatrix} \).
14. The orthogonal basis for the column space is \( \left\{ \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ -1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 0 \\ 3 \\ -3 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 0 \\ 2 \\ 2 \\ -2 \end{bmatrix} \right\} \).
