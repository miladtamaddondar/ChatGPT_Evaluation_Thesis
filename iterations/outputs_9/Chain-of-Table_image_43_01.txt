1. Write \( U = [u_1\ u_2] = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \).
2. Compute \( U^T U = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ -\frac{2}{3} & \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \).
3. Compute \( U U^T = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & -\frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} & \frac{2}{3} \\ -\frac{2}{3} & \frac{2}{3} & \frac{1}{3} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} & -\frac{2}{9} & -\frac{4}{9} \\ -\frac{2}{9} & \frac{5}{9} & \frac{8}{9} \\ -\frac{4}{9} & \frac{8}{9} & \frac{5}{9} \end{bmatrix} \).
4. Compute \( (U U^T) y = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{8}{9} & -\frac{2}{9} & -\frac{4}{9} \\ -\frac{2}{9} & \frac{5}{9} & \frac{8}{9} \\ -\frac{4}{9} & \frac{8}{9} & \frac{5}{9} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 8 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix} \).
5. The projection \( \mathrm{proj}_W y = (U U^T) y = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix} \).
